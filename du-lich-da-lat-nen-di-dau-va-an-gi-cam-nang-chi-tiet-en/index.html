🌄 Da Lat - the city of flowers, not only stands out with its beautiful natural scenery but is also a rich culinary paradise. If you are planning a trip to Da Lat, this article will help you answer the question: “Where should you go and what to eat when traveling to Da Lat?”with suggestions for places to visit and the most attractive delicacies today.

Which destinations should you explore when traveling to Da Lat?

Da Lat is a convergence of many famous landscapes, suitable for both nature lovers, families, or groups of young people. Below is a list of places you cannot miss:

🌅 Xuan Huong Lake - The bustling center of the flower city

Photo: Xuan Huong Lake - Dalat Tourism

Xuan Huong Lake is a familiar destination for all visitors to Da Lat. With fresh space and poetic scenery, the lake brings a wonderful feeling of relaxation.

📸 Suggested activities: Cycling around the lake, duck boating, taking sunset photos.

💡 Note: Come early in the morning or late in the evening to enjoy the cool air and beautiful scenery.

💕 Valley of Love - Paradise for couples

Photo: Valley of Love - Dalat Travel

With green space, flower paths, and clear blue lakes, Valley of Love is an ideal destination for couples looking for romance.

🌟 Highlights: Flower maze, lake cruise, entertainment area.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Suitable for: Couples and families traveling with children.

🌸 Lam Vien Square - Where many cultural events take place

Photo: Lam Vien Square - Dalat Tourism

The square stands out with two big symbols: wild sunflowers and giant artichokes, creating an ideal virtual living space.

📸 Activities: Take check-in photos, participate in festivals, have fun outdoors.

🛍️ Near Da Lat night market– convenient for shopping for specialties as gifts.

🍃 Cau Dat Tea Hill - Experience peaceful nature

Photo: Cau Dat Tea Hill - Da Lat Tourism

Not only is it a famous tea production place, Cau Dat Tea Hill also attracts tourists with its green space, fresh atmosphere and the opportunity to visit the traditional tea processing process.

📸 Activities: Sightseeing, virtual live photography, tea picking.

🌅 The most beautiful time: Dawn to catch the golden sunlight.

What dishes should you eat when traveling to Da Lat? Top delicious specialties not to be missed

Da Lat cuisine is diverse, from street food to traditional specialties. Below are the dishes you should try when coming to Da Lat:

🥞 Banh can shumai - Typical rustic dish of Da Lat

Photo: Banh can with meatballs - Da Lat travel

📍 Suggested address: Nha Chung cake shop - No. 13 Nha Chung, Ward 3.

🍽️ Special flavor: Crispy cake, combined with rich dipping sauce and delicious shumai topping.

🍢 Grilled spring rolls - Famous specialty with unique flavor

Photo: Nem Nuong - Dalat Travel

📍 Place to try: Ba Hung Restaurant - 254 Phan Dinh Phung.

🌿 Enjoy: Grilled spring rolls with fresh raw vegetables, thin rice paper and rich dipping sauce.

🍧 Avocado ice cream - Cool dessert for summer days

Photo: Bo Ice Cream - Da Lat Travel

🍦 Should try at: Thanh Thao avocado ice cream - 76 Nguyen Van Troi.

🥑 Attractive point: Smooth and creamy ice cream mixed with sweet fresh butter.

🌟Good tips to make your trip to Da Lat more complete

Below are some experiences to help you have the most convenient and comfortable trip to Da Lat

📅Detailed planning

➤ Determine destinations by area to save time

➤ Prioritize your visit time in the early morning and late afternoon to avoid harsh sunlight

🛵Motorbike rental - popular vehicle

➤ Easy to move, reasonable rental price

➤ Be sure to carefully check the vehicle before renting and always wear a helmet

🧥Prepare suitable clothes

➤ Da Lat is quite cold at night, you should bring warm clothes

➤ Sports shoes or soft shoes for ease of movement on mountainous terrain

📍Suggested itinerary to travel to Da Lat in 1 day

☀️Morning

➤ Visit Xuan Huong Lake, relax and drink coffee by the lake

➤ Visit Lam Vien Square to take virtual photos

🌤️Afternoon

➤ Valley of Love or Cau Dat tea hill

➤ Enjoy avocado ice cream or famous grilled spring rolls

🌙Dark

➤ Dinner at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

➤ Visit the night market to buy gifts and enjoy street specialties
