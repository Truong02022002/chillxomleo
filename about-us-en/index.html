Our Story

The Story of Xom Leo – Da Lat BBQ with the Best Valley View

Tucked away on a windy hillside, Xom Leo offers more than just BBQ. We bring you a symphony of mist, glowing charcoal, and the most vibrant sunsets in Da Lat.

Touch your emotions,
awaken your taste buds.

Da Lat has countless BBQ spots, but Xom Leo carves its own unique path. It's a place where you can grill sizzling beef while gazing up at the fiery sunset sky.

In the chilly mountain air, nothing warms the heart faster than a glowing fire and true friends raising a toast. Our completely open-space design ensures that no matter where you sit, the "Train View" and the glittering greenhouse valley remain perfectly in sight.
