What to Eat in Da Lat?

Photo: What to Eat in Da Lat?
Table of Contents

Da Lat not only captivates tourists with its poetic natural scenery and cool climate but also with its rich and unique cuisine. Let's explore delicious dishes and unique culinary addresses in Da Lat to make your trip more complete.

1. 🥘 Chicken Heart Moist Cake

Photo: Da Lat

Chicken intestine wet cake is a unique combination of soft and smooth wet cake, delicious chewy chicken intestine and sweet and sour fish sauce. The dish is simple but has a very unique flavor that can only be found in Da Lat.

Famous restaurant: Quan Long.

📍 Address: Alley 202, Phan Dinh Phung, Da Lat.

Reference price: 30,000 - 50,000 VND/portion.

2. 🍡 Grilled Rice Paper – “Vietnamese Pizza”

Photo: Da Lat

Da Lat grilled rice paper stands out with its crispy crust and filling including eggs, cheese, sausage, minced meat... This is a snack not to be missed when walking around the streets of Da Lat in the evening.

Famous restaurant: Aunt Dinh's Grilled Rice Paper.

📍 Address: 26 Hoang Dieu, Ward 5, Da Lat.

Reference price: 20,000 - 35,000 VND/piece.

3. 🍢 Xap Xap – Beef jerky salad version

Photo: Da Lat

Xap Xap is a familiar street food to Da Lat people, similar to Southern dried beef salad. The main ingredients include shredded papaya, beef jerky, sweet and sour fish sauce and crispy roasted peanuts.

Famous restaurant: Xap Xap Cau Ong Dao.

📍 Address: Ong Dao bridge area, center of Da Lat.

Reference price: 15,000 - 25,000 VND/portion.

4. 🥟 Banh Can – Warm flavor on a cold day

Photo: Da Lat

Banh can has a crispy crust, with a variety of fillings such as eggs, shrimp, minced meat, served with fish sauce or seasoned fish sauce mixed with grated mango and green onions. This dish is loved because of its simplicity but very flavorful taste.

Famous restaurant: Banh Can Nha Chung.

📍 Address: 13 Nha Chung, Ward 3, Da Lat.

Reference price: 25,000 - 40,000 VND/portion.

5. 🧆 Nem Nuong

Photo: Da Lat

Grilled spring rolls are made from ground pork, rolled with rice paper, raw vegetables and dipped in rich peanut sauce. This is a popular dish and is very popular with tourists.

Famous restaurant: Nem Nuong Ba Hung.

📍 Address: 254 Phan Dinh Phung, Ward 2, Da Lat.

Reference price: 40,000 - 60,000 VND/portion.

6. 🥩 Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop – Enjoy delicious grilled food

Photo: Da Lat

If you want to enjoy grilled food in a cozy space, come to Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop. The restaurant is famous for its richly marinated grilled dishes, unique hot pots and extremely chill views, very suitable for gathering with friends and relatives.

📍 Address: 113 Huynh Tan Phat, Ward 11, Da Lat.

7. ☕ Enjoy coffee at shops with beautiful views

Photo: Da Lat

Da Lat has no shortage of cafes with beautiful spaces. You can admire the misty mountain or city views while enjoying a hot cup of coffee.

Featured restaurant:

Me Linh Coffee Garden.

The Wilder-nest Coffee.

Reference price: 30,000 - 70,000 VND/glass.

8. 🍨 Da Lat Avocado Ice Cream – Sweet dessert

Photo: Da Lat

Avocado ice cream is a famous dessert, with creamy smooth butter combined with cool coconut cream. This is a dish not to be missed when coming to the city of flowers.

Famous shop: Thanh Thao Avocado Ice Cream.

📍 Address: 76 Nguyen Van Troi, Ward 2, Da Lat.

Reference price: 20,000 - 40,000 VND/portion.

Da Lat not only captivates tourists with its beautiful scenery but also with its rich and diverse cuisine. Whether it's street food or cozy barbecue parties at 🔥 Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop, you will definitely have unforgettable culinary experiences. Plan now to enjoy all these delicious dishes on your journey to Da Lat! 🌸
