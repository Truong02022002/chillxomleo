*Tuyen Lam LakeDa Lat* is an artificial freshwater lake located peacefully on the outskirts of the city, near the area*Prenn Pass***. With a large area, charming landscape and cool air all year round, this place quickly becomes a favorite destination for tourists every time they visit the city of flowers. The natural scenery blending between mountains and forests and a quiet lake creates an ideal space to relax and enjoy the pristine beauty.

Not only possessing poetic beauty, Tuyen Lam Lake Da Lat also offers many attractive experiences such as boating, camping, picnicking, fishing or simply walking along the lake to breathe fresh air. Besides, visitors also have the opportunity to enjoy local specialties at famous restaurants scattered around the lake area, creating a complete journey of discovery in both scenery and cuisine.

1. Introduction to Tuyen Lam Lake in Da Lat

*Tuyen Lam Lake Da Lat*located at Tuyen Lam Lake National Tourist Area, Ward 4, Da Lat City. This is the city's largest artificial lake with a total area of ​​up to 360 hectares. The lake is open to visitors all day and is completely free, very convenient for visitors to experience at any time of the year.

The space around the lake is covered with vast pine forests and green flora, creating a charming natural scene, blending between mountains and clear blue water. Therefore, this place is considered the "green lung" of Da Lat, an ideal destination to relax, breathe fresh air and immerse yourself in nature.

According to local people, this land was discovered by a Frenchman in the 1930s. During the resistance war against America, this place used to be a revolutionary base called Quang Trung District or Suoi Tia District.

In 1987, after the country was unified, Lam Dong Irrigation Company built a 235m long dam to block Suoi Tia, creating a large artificial lake. Initially, the lake was named Quang Trung Lake, later it was renamed Tuyen Lam Lake as it is today.

With special historical and landscape value, in 1988, this area was recognized as a National Historical-Cultural Monument. By 2017, the lake continued to be upgraded by becoming a National Tourist Area, attracting millions of domestic and foreign tourists. Currently, Tuyen Lam Lake is directly managed and developed by SAM Da Lat, becoming an indispensable destination in the journey to discover dreamy Da Lat.

2. Which season is best to travel to Tuyen Lam Lake?

The most ideal time to visit Tuyen Lam Lake in Da Lat is during the dry season, which lasts from November to June of the following year. During this period, the weather is dry, not too cold and has little rain, very suitable for participating in outdoor activities such as camping, fishing, boating, or capturing beautiful "virtual life" moments by lakes and pine forests.

However, in the rainy season (from May to September), Tuyen Lam Lake takes on a different, quiet and poetic look. Although the weather is a bit chilly and there is scattered rain, this creates a peaceful space - ideal for you to relax, enjoy hot coffee by the window, or gather with friends around the grill or steaming hot pot.

No matter what season you visit, Tuyen Lam Lake Da Lat always brings interesting experiences and unique emotions to tourists who love nature and tranquility.

3. Activities you can't miss when traveling to Tuyen Lam Lake, Da Lat

Not only outstanding with its peaceful beauty and fresh air, Tuyen Lam Lake Da Lat is also a place that brings diverse and memorable experiences. Below are activities that tourists must definitely try when arriving in this green paradise.

3.1. Excursing to Tuyen Lam Lake by yacht - Experience relaxation in nature

Photo: Tuyen Lam Lake

Walking around Tuyen Lam Lake on a cruise ship is a great way to fully enjoy the beauty of this place. Every morning, the lake surface is as smooth as a mirror, reflecting the pine trees and clear blue sky. Visitors can leisurely sit on the boat, listen to the gentle sound of water lapping and enjoy the cool breeze filtering through each hair.

The season when maple leaves change color or clear days are the best times for you to enjoy the scenery. Boat rental prices range from 300,000 - 500,000 VND, can carry about 10-15 people, very suitable for groups of friends or families.

3.2. SUP and kayak to explore the wild beauty of Tuyen Lam Lake

Photo: Introducing Tuyen Lam Lake in Dalat

An activity imbued with the spirit of discovery that young tourists love at Tuyen Lam Lake is kayaking or SUP. Small boats help you weave into the corners of the lake, along the pine forests or red maple forests that are turning leaves in late autumn.

Under the guidance of an experienced person, you will both learn boating skills and immerse yourself in the vast space of lakes and mountains. For those who love nature and like new feelings, this is a "worth the money" experience.

3.3. Fishing at Tuyen Lam Lake - Relax while enjoying the spoils

Photo: The beauty of Tuyen Lam Lake

Without much investment, just a simple fishing rod, you can experience free fishing at Tuyen Lam Lake. The lake has many types of freshwater fish, and the quiet space will help you relax and immerse yourself in the slow pace of life in the mountains and forests.

The special thing is that you can ask the surrounding restaurants to immediately process the booty you catch into fresh, rustic dishes - an experience that is both fun and rich in local identity.

3.4. Camp and picnic on the banks of Tuyen Lam Lake - Enjoy the cool green space

Photo: Excursing to Tuyen Lam Lake by yacht

Tuyen Lam Lake is one of the most ideal camping and picnic locations in Da Lat. With many flat open spaces by the lake, you can easily set up a tent, organize a BBQ, light a campfire and watch the moon and stars beside the calm water.

In the morning, when the dew was still lingering on the lake, opening the tent saw the sunrise shining through the trees - that would be an unforgettable moment on the trip. Camping costs are not too expensive, ranging around 1,200,000 VND/night for a group of 5–7 people.

3.5. Climbing Mount Pinhatt - Panoramic view of Tuyen Lam Lake from above

Photo: Rowing on Tuyen Lam Lake

If you are someone who likes to exercise and explore, try climbing Mount Pinhatt - a high mountain in the Tuyen Lam Lake area. The road is not too crowded, still retains its wildness, very suitable for backpackers and people who like unique experiences.

From the top of the mountain, you can see a panoramic view of the vast lake and Da Lat city in the distance, hazy in the mist. Prepare comfortable clothes, drinks, snacks and don't forget your camera - because the scenery here will definitely make you want to keep it forever.

3.6. Explore Tuyen Lam Lake spillway - A hidden virtual living corner

Photo: Fishing at Tuyen Lam Lake

Tuyen Lam Lake Spillway is not a popular tourist destination, but it is a "hidden" coordinates for those who love tranquility and wild scenery. The dam is located deep in the pine forest, surrounded by reeds and whispering green trees, creating a romantic scene like in the movies.

If you are someone who likes to take photos, this place is definitely a great "background" with soft lighting, gurgling water sounds and fresh atmosphere. Come early morning or late afternoon to catch the best moment.

3.7. Watching the sunset at Tuyen Lam Lake - An unforgettable romantic moment

Photo: Camping at Tuyen Lam Lake

Sunset at Tuyen Lam Lake is considered a gift from nature. As the sun gradually sets behind the mountains, the last light of the day dyes the lake and pine forest golden - all creating a dreamy, emotional scene.

Many tourists choose to come here just to sit quietly by the lake, sip a cup of coffee, or simply enjoy a moment of peace amidst the poetic nature of Da Lat.

3.8. Check-in at Tuyen Lam Lake wooden bridge - Famous virtual living symbol

Photo: Pinhatt peak, Tuyen Lam Lake

One of the destinations not to be missed when coming to Tuyen Lam Lake, Da Lat is the wooden bridge located next to the lavender field. The rustic bridge, located in the middle of a quiet space, has a very unique beauty - both romantic and intimate.

Here, you can freely pose with the surrounding scenery of mountains, clouds and green forests. Don't forget to choose the right outfit to take stunning photos!

3.9. Try your hand at the golf course by Tuyen Lam Lake - Experience classy sports

Photo: Spillway of Tuyen Lam Lake

Located right on the shore of the lake, Sam Tuyen Lam golf course is an ideal place for tourists who love aristocratic sports. With an international standard 18-hole design, open space and diverse terrain, this place promises to bring a challenging and exciting experience.

You can focus on playing golf while looking out at the surrounding blue lake and mountains - a wonderfully balanced experience between exercise and relaxation.

6. What to do near Tuyen Lam Lake? Tourist destinations should be combined with sightseeing

The area around Tuyen Lam Lake is home to many famous tourist attractions, suitable for combining sightseeing in a day or half a day. Below are prominent destinations:

-

Truc Lam Zen Monastery

👉 One of the largest and most beautiful Zen monasteries in Vietnam. Located on Phuong Hoang Hill, from here you can look down on the panoramic view of romantic Tuyen Lam Lake.

-

La Phong tourist area

👉 "Miniature Japan" in the heart of Da Lat, featuring changing maple forests, Koi fish ponds, and unique architecture.

-

Sculpture Tunnel

👉 The tourist area simulates the history of Da Lat through sculpted clay works, very suitable for checking in and taking photos.

-

Datanla Waterfall

👉 A place for adventure lovers: slides through the forest, swinging over waterfalls, ziplining... only about 4km from the lake.

-

Valley of Love

👉 A romantic destination with many beautiful landscapes, flower gardens and amusement parks suitable for the whole family.

7. Some common questions when traveling to Tuyen Lam Lake

7.1. Does Tuyen Lam Lake have a cable car?

Photo: Sunset at Tuyen Lam Lake

✅ Yes. Visitors can see the scenery of Tuyen Lam Lake from above with the Robin Da Lat cable car– connecting from Robin Hill to the area near Truc Lam Zen Monastery.

-

Address: Robin Hill, Ward 3, Da Lat

-

Opening hours: 07:30 – 17:00

-

Ticket price: 80,000 - 100,000 VND/person

The cable car trip will give you a panoramic view of Tuyen Lam Lake from above - an experience worth trying at least once when coming to Da Lat.

7.2. Is there rescue in the Tuyen Lam Lake area?

Photo: Play Golf at Tuyen Lam Lake

✅ Yes. Tuyen Lam Lake tourist area has a management board and a rescue team on constant duty, ensuring the safety of visitors, especially during activities such as SUP, kayaking or swimming in the lake.

📌 Suggestion: If you go in a large group, especially with young children, you should bring personal life jackets and follow safety instructions when participating in water activities.
