When mentioning *Da Lat*, everyone immediately thinks of the cool climate all year round, the whispering pine trees and the fog spreading every early morning. But not only the dreamy scenery, Da Lat cuisine is also what attracts tourists - especially the Da Lat grilled restaurants scattered in the middle of the mountain town. In the cold weather, sitting by the fire, enjoying hot grilled dishes with friends and relatives is an experience that everyone should try at least once.

In 2025, if you are planning to explore the foggy city and want to find a delicious Da Lat grilled restaurant, don't miss the address loved by many food lovers: Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop– a true "delicious food and chill" destination!

1. Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop – Da Lat grill shop with a very chill train view

Located at 113 Huynh Tan Phat, Ward 11, City. Da Lat, Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop is one of the dining places in Da Lat 2025 that is highly appreciated by many young people and tourists. Not only does it impress with its diverse menu and reasonable prices, but it also stands out with its extremely unique space: sunset view and train huntingunique in the heart of Da Lat.

Under the brilliant sunset, the train whistle sounds from afar, you sip grilled meat and chat with your group of friends - a typical Da Lat experience, full of chill and romance.

1.1. Da Lat's chill grilled restaurant space in the middle of dreamy mountains and forests

Photo: Da Lat grilled restaurant

What makes many diners "fall in love" with Xom Leo the first time they visit is the space. The shop is designed in a rustic style with modern touches, bold in the mountains, but still brings a feeling of closeness and comfort. You can choose to sit outside with warm yellow lights, or private tent corners for couples or small families.

Open, airy space and harmony with nature help meals become more relaxing and pleasant than ever. Whether you are a check-in lover or simply want to find a place to rest and eat with your loved ones, Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop can satisfy you.

1.2.Da Lat grilled restaurant menu is diverse - Rich in mountain town flavor

Photo: Grill & Chill Xom Leo - Da Lat grilled restaurant

One of the reasons why Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shopbecomes a popular Da Lat delicious grilled restaurant is thanks to its rich menu, suitable for many people's tastes. From American beef rolled with enoki mushrooms, grilled chicken feet with salt and chili, to seafood dishes such as shrimp, squid, octopus, grilled sturgeon**,... all are carefully selected from fresh ingredients every day.

Not stopping there, the restaurant also carefully invests in exclusive dipping sauce– mixed according to a unique recipe, creating a "you'll remember" flavor that you can hardly find anywhere else. All dishes are seasoned to taste and grilled to a delicious aroma that makes everyone passing by take a look.

In addition, the menu also includes fresh vegetables as a side dish, which helps balance the taste buds, making the meal not boring but always harmonious and attractive.

**1.3. The grill restaurant in Da Lat has reasonable prices and dedicated service

Photo: Space for Baking Shop & Chill Xom Leo - Da Lat grilled restaurant

Despite possessing a prime location, beautiful space and rich menu, the prices at *Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop* are very affordable. From only 200,000 - 250,000 VND/person, you can enjoy a full grilled meal with many special dishes.

The restaurant regularly has attractive promotions, especially free table setup when you reserve a table in advance via fanpage or hotline.

The staff at the restaurant are highly appreciated for their enthusiasm, friendliness and agility. Whether you travel alone, in a couple or in a large group, you will receive attentive service from A to Z, ensuring the most complete experience.
