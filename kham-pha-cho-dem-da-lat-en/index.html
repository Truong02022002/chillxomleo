*Da Lat Night Market*, also known by its popular name *Am Phu Market*, is not only a famous sightseeing destination but also a place that clearly shows the daily life and typical culture of the people of the dreamy mountain town.

If you want to feel the bustle and crowd of *Da Lat* when night falls, you should definitely visit the bustling night market right in the city center. Here, there are not only hundreds of stalls of all kinds, but also a culinary paradise that makes it difficult for visitors to resist.

1. Some details about Da Lat night market

*Da Lat Night Market* also has the familiar nickname "*Am Phu Market*". This curious name originates from the time when the market was first formed. At that time, there were only a few street vendors selling food late at night, flickering under the light of oil lamps in the cold night mist - creating a dim space like the "underworld", from which the unique name was born.

Nowadays, the night market has become a familiar destination every night. From around 5pm, stalls start to set up, being most vibrant around 7pm until late. This is the time when visitors can immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Da Lat, shopping and enjoying local specialties.

2. Where is Da Lat Night Market located & how to get there

- Address:Nguyen Thi Minh Khai Street, Ward 1, City. Da Lat

The night market is located right in the center of Da Lat, convenient for both residents and tourists to visit. If you stay in the central area or near Ward 2 and Ward 10, you can walk to the market. For those who live farther away, you can rent a motorbike (price from 100,000 - 170,000 VND/day), take a taxi or call a motorbike taxi, which are all very convenient.

Starting from Xuan Huong Lake, just cross a small bridge and a roundabout to immediately see the bustling night market. The parking lot here includes both residential and public parking areas and is very easy to find.

Suggestion:Refer to the list of quality car companies going to Da Lat to plan your trip in advance.

3. What to eat at Da Lat night market? 8 must-try specialties

If you come to Da Lat night market and don't know which dish to enjoy, below is a list of "must try" dishes that cannot be missed:

3.1 Grilled rice paper at Da Lat night market

Photo: Da Lat Night Market

Known as "Da Lat pizza", this cake has a crispy crust, topped with a mixture of toppings including onions, beef jerky, eggs, burnt shrimp, cheese,... creating a unique delicious taste. Price is only from 15,000 - 25,000 VND/piece.

3.2 Da Lat night market avocado ice cream

Photo: Da Lat Night Market

Avocado ice cream at the night market uses fresh, smooth waxy butter, blended with sweet cream, creating a rich yet cool dessert - very suitable for the cold Da Lat climate.

3.3 Banh canh and vermicelli noodles at Da Lat night market

Photo: Grilled Rice Paper - Da Lat Night Market

Amid the coldness of the mountain town, there's nothing better than enjoying a hot, rich bowl of banh canh or vermicelli noodles. The broth is naturally sweet from the bones, with a little satay added to warm the heart.

3.4 Snails at Da Lat night market

Photo: Avocado Ice Cream - Da Lat Night Market

One of the familiar images at Da Lat night market is the burden of steaming snails. Attractive snails and clams are prepared on the spot, in all kinds of ways: stir-fried, grilled, steamed... with a mouth-watering aroma.

3.5 Da Lat night market shumai bread

Photo: Bun Rieu at Da Lat night market

A familiar dish that pleases diners with its soft and fragrant meatballs, sweet bone broth, and served with hot, crispy bread. Prices range from 30,000 - 45,000 VND.

##### 3.6 Fruit shakes at Da Lat night market

Da Lat is famous for strawberries and other fruits. Fruit shakes at the night market are often mixed with shrimp salt, sugar, chili... creating an attractive sweet and sour flavor. A glass of fruit shake costs only 15,000 - 20,000 VND.

3.7 Hot soy milk at Da Lat night market

Photo: Snails at Da Lat night market

The highlight of the night market cuisine is the hot, fragrant cups of soy milk. Combining with cakes or bread will help you keep your body warm after walking around the night market.

##### 3.8 Hot tofu at Da Lat night market

Amid the chilly cold of the mountain town, a bowl of hot, soft tofu, flavored with fragrant ginger sugar water, will be a great choice to end the market walk. The gentle sweetness and warmth spread from this rustic dish will definitely warm your heart.

4. What's interesting about Da Lat Night Market for shopping?

If you've been to Da Lat night market and haven't stopped by the clothing, agricultural products, souvenirs stalls... then you're missing out. Not only is it a place to enjoy street food, the market is also a shopping paradise for visitors with a variety of items at affordable prices, suitable to bring home as gifts or simply to reward yourself with a small, beautiful item.

4.1 Cheap second-hand clothes at Da Lat night market

Photo: Da Lat night market shumai bread

A special feature at Da Lat night market are the stalls selling second-hand clothes, where you can find used but very new clothes at super economical prices. In addition, the market also has many new clothing stores with all styles - from dynamic and individual to gentle and vintage. This is truly a favorite destination for fashionistas who like to "hunt for unique items".

*Tip:*If you choose carefully, you can "pick up" branded items for the price of just a snack!

4.2 Lovely souvenirs at Da Lat night market

Photo: Fruit Shake Dalat night market

The night market is also an ideal place for you to buy small gifts with Da Lat style. From keychains, brocade bags, handmade bracelets to products made from dried flowers, silk, embroidery... all are sold here. Whether you want to buy an exquisite gift or simply a souvenir of your trip, the night market always has options available that are both beautiful and affordable.

4.3 Agricultural products and fruit specialties at Da Lat night market

Photo: Hot soy milk at Da Lat night market

When mentioning Da Lat, we must mention famous fresh agricultural products such as strawberries, avocado, crispy persimmons... At the night market, these products are sold everywhere, both easy to find and easy to buy. If you come at dawn, you can also see traders bringing newly harvested vegetables to the market - at this time the prices are often cheaper and the freshness is superior compared to during the day.

*Note:*Choose to buy at reputable stores, ask carefully about the origin and preservation method if you intend to bring it home as a gift.

4.4 Jam and dry goods - Specialties bought as gifts at Da Lat night market

Photo: Hot tofu at Da Lat night market

Jams are one of the most popular items at Da Lat night market. You will be "overwhelmed" by dozens of attractive fruit jams: strawberry jam, persimmon jam, mango jam, dried sweet potatoes, crispy dried vegetables... Not only is it eye-catching, but the flavor is also delicious, suitable as gifts for relatives after the trip.

*Pro tip:*Try a little first to choose the right flavor for your taste, and don't forget to check the expiration date if buying in bulk.

5. Experience going to Da Lat night market for first-time visitors

To make your journey to explore Da Lat night market more complete, especially for those who come here for the first time, you should refer to a few experiences below to prepare better!

5.1 Until what time is Da Lat Night Market open?

Photo: Da Lat night market souvenirs

The night market starts bustling around 5pm and usually lasts until 4-5am the next morning. Evening is the most ideal time for you to immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere, watch the streets light up and enjoy delicious food.

5.2 Where to park the car when going to Da Lat night market?

Photo: Souvenirs from Da Lat night market

You can park your motorbike right at the main parking lot at the market gate for a fee of about 10,000 VND/trip. In addition, if this area is full, you can find a nearby parking lot such as the Da Lat market roundabout area, Tung Nghia parking lot (near Hôtel Colline hotel) or park your car at TASTY cafe and then walk to the market, which is also very convenient.

5.3 Where should you stay to conveniently visit Da Lat night market?

Photo: Agricultural products at Da Lat night market

5.4 Suggestions for "cool" virtual living corners at Da Lat night market

Photo: Da Lat night market specialties

Not only is it a place to shop and eat, Da Lat night market also has many extremely eye-catching photography corners, suitable for virtual life believers. You can take advantage of check-in at points such as:

- Stairs walking from Hoa Binh area to the market

- Da Lat market slope when the lights come on

- "Hong Kong area next to Da Lat market"– right at the stairs behind the market

- Da Lat market main gate– especially sparkling in the evening

Each corner brings a background rich in antiquity mixed with a bit of modernity, very "photogenic"!

5.5 A few small notes to have a complete experience at Da Lat night market

Photo: Da Lat night market

- Be careful with your belongings: The night market is often crowded, especially on weekends or peak tourist seasons. You should wear a chest bag and avoid carrying a lot of cash or valuables.

- Haggling skillfully: Bargaining is very common at the market. Don't be afraid to negotiate to get the most reasonable price for the item you like.

- Ask the price before eating: The night market food area is quite vibrant, many restaurants do not clearly list prices. Therefore, you should carefully ask the price of the food before ordering to avoid being ripped off.

- Check the quality of specialties: If you plan to buy specialties such as jam, dried fruit or handicrafts as gifts, you should pay attention to the expiration date, packaging, color and flavor to choose a quality product.

- Be careful when taking photos with mascots: On weekends, many cartoon characters appear on the walking streets around the market. Although it looks very fun, you need to ask clearly about the cost of taking photos to avoid losing money unfairly.
