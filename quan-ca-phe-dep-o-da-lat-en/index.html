Da Lat has no shortage of poetic cafes, where you can find peace amidst pure nature, enjoying a fragrant cup of coffee amid a romantic setting. From Cheo Veooo, Tung's Corner to Pura Vida Da Lat - each restaurant has its own unique features that make anyone who visits once never want to leave.

1. On Cloudy Hill****–****The most beautiful cloud hunting coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address: Cua Cay Mai, Xuan Tho commune

📞 Phone number: 0339337693

🕗 Opening hours: 05:00 - 21:00

If you want to hunt clouds and enjoy the fresh air in Da Lat, Cloudy Hill Coffee Shop is an option not to be missed. Located in a prime location, the restaurant has a wide view, helping you easily immerse yourself in the dim dawns with a sea of ​​floating clouds. The restaurant's menu is diverse, from coffee dishes to other drinks, allowing you to freely choose according to your preferences.

2. Quyen Coffee – Beautiful and chill coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address:No. 7 Co Giang, Ward 9

📞 Phone number: 0965521109

🕗 Opening hours: 06:00 - 15:00

Quyen Coffee is one of the beautiful coffee shops in Da Lat famous for its small, cozy space. This is the right place to enjoy a peaceful moment with a delicious cup of coffee and characteristic herbal tea flavor.

3. Cheo Veooo Coffee Shop - Beautiful coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address:Da Chien Alley, 116 Hung Vuong, Ward 11

📞 Phone number: 0938716394

Cheo Veooo is one of the cafes with beautiful views in Da Lat, located halfway up the mountain with a nostalgic and peaceful space. Here, you can hunt clouds and watch the sunset, creating an unforgettable experience when coming to the city of flowers.

4. Co Ba Egg Coffee Shop - Famous egg coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address:105 Hai Ba Trung, Ward 6

📞 Phone number: 0945209079

Co Ba egg coffee shop is a familiar stop for many tourists when coming to Da Lat. The shop has a minimalist, cozy space, with gentle music to help you relax and enjoy a delicious cup of egg coffee.

5. The Diff House's - Beautiful, romantic coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address:66/3 Dat Lang, Xuan Truong commune

📞 Phone number: 0917493992

🕗 Opening hours: 05:30 - 18:00

The Diff House's is known as a beautiful and romantic coffee shop in Da Lat, with a minimalist design style, cozy space and great cloud hunting opportunities, bringing relaxation to guests who come to enjoy coffee.

6. Black Garlic Cafe - Unique coffee shop in Da Lat

📍 Address:318 Bui Thi Xuan, Ward 8

📞 Phone number: 0968115005

🕗 Opening hours: 07:00 - 22:30

Black Garlic Cafe is a unique coffee shop in the center of Da Lat, attracting young people with its unique black garlic coffee. The shop has a beautiful view, spacious space, very suitable for you to enjoy cold mornings with a cup of fragrant coffee.

7. Tung's corner - Da Lat coffee shop to chill, watch the clouds, hunt for beautiful sunrises

📍Address:3/4 Street, Ward 3

📞Phone number:0772500212

If you are looking for a chill cafe in Da Lat to watch the clouds float by, "Tung's Corner" is a name not to be missed. Located in the middle of fresh nature, the shop owns an open space with many cool virtual photography angles. From the front yard, balcony to backyard, everywhere you can watch the brilliant sunrise or romantic sunset moment.

With a rustic and bold Da Lat design, Tung's Corner always brings a feeling of peace and gentleness to those who visit.

8. Homuda – Da Lat cafe with Japanese style as beautiful as a movie

📍Address:40 Mai Anh Dao, Ward 8

📞Phone number:0933466380

🕗Opening hours:08:00 - 19:00

Homuda is a beautiful cafe in Da Lat with Japanese characteristics, providing visitors with a peaceful and sophisticated space. Every small corner here is decorated in a minimalist yet poetic style, from the small garden, outdoor corner to the rustic wooden attic.

In addition to a great space to check-in, Homuda also serves a variety of attractive drinks, from pure coffee to Japanese-standard flower tea and matcha. This is the ideal place for those who love the "wabi-sabi" style and the gentleness of the land of the rising sun in the heart of Da Lat.

9. Pura Vida Da Lat – HOT cafe in Da Lat with a very chill open space

📍Address:Alley 108 Hung Vuong, Ward 11

📞Phone number:0936115171

🕗Opening hours:08:00 - 20:00

Pura Vida Da Lat is one of the HOT cafes in Da Lat thanks to its spacious, airy space and harmony with nature. With more than 90% of the area being outdoors, the restaurant always receives natural sunlight and fresh air, extremely suitable for those who want to find relaxation and comfort.

At Pura Vida, you can sip coffee while watching the romantic sunset from the distant hillsides - an unforgettable experience when coming to Da Lat.

📞Phone number:0979888850

🕗Opening hours:

-

Monday - Thursday: 08:00 - 22:00

-

The shop has an extremely romantic valley view, ideal for checking in with your loved one or simply enjoying the peace of the mountain town.

11. Emai Da Lat – Delicious cafe in Da Lat with a romantic Mediterranean atmosphere

📍Address:88 Hoang Hoa Tham, Ward 10

📞Phone number:0263 357 7246

🕗Opening hours:07:30 – 22:00 (closed every Thursday)

Émai Da Lat is not only a delicious cafe in Da Lat, but also a place that offers a true Italian culinary experience in a sophisticated, cozy space. From the flower gate leading to the small yard covered with green trees, every detail is meticulously cared for.

Minimalist interior, soft lighting and faint herbal scent create an extremely romantic space, very suitable for dates or enjoying tea alone in the rainy afternoon of Da Lat.

12. Summer Garden – Beautiful cafe in Da Lat filled with colorful flowers

📍Address:No. 27 Alley 1 Dang Thai Than, Ward 3

📞Phone number:0936525327

🕗Opening hours:08:00 - 19:00

True to its name, Summer Garden is a beautiful cafe in Da Lat that makes everyone fall in love at first sight. From the hibiscus-lined entrance to the lush green garden behind, this place gives the feeling of being lost in a fairy tale garden.

The shop is designed to be open, blending with nature and having many views of the beautiful valley - an ideal place to enjoy a hot cup of coffee in the cool air of the mountain town.

13. Binh Minh Oi Coffee Shop – Da Lat coffee shop with classic, rustic style

📍Address:89 Hoang Hoa Tham, Ward 10

📞Phone number:0925903634

Binh Minh Oi Coffee Shop is a beautiful coffee shop in Da Lat with a classic style with rustic wooden furniture, a garden filled with daisies and a space tinged with old colors.

This place is especially suitable for those who love nostalgia, peace and want gentle vintage photos. A cup of hot coffee in the middle of a sunny garden, the sound of birds chirping - that is Da Lat in the hearts of many people.

Or you can choose Tung's Corner (Street 3/4, Ward 3):

Or visit Cheo Veooo(116 Hung Vuong, Ward 11):
