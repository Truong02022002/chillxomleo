1. Robin Hill Da Lat - A panoramic view of the dreamy city from above

Located on the romantic Prenn Pass, Da Lat Robin Hill - also known as Robin Cable Car Hill - is an ideal stop for tourists who want to fully admire the poetic beauty of the "foggy city". With a cable car line about 2,267m long across the green pine forest, this place connects Robin Hill and Truc Lam Zen Monastery - two prominent destinations of Da Lat tourism.

Not only attractive by its majestic natural scenery, Robin Hill also contains a special historical story. Before 1975, this area used to be the Tan Lac artillery battle - a strategic position in the defense system of our army during the resistance war against the US. After peace, Robin Hill was planned and built into the first cable car system in Da Lat, quickly becoming a favorite tourist destination for tourists from near and far.

Standing on the top of the hill, you will take in the panoramic view of the romantic Da Lat city with red-tiled roof houses hidden among pine forests, greenhouses growing colorful flowers, and ancient villas tinged with time in the mist. Experiencing the cable car from above brings a sense of relaxation, helping you "relax" into nature and enjoy the fresh air typical of the plateau.

Not only is it an ideal viewing spot, Robin Hill is also an extremely hot check-in location for young people. This place is suitable for outdoor activities such as picnics, picnics, or simply relaxing after the journey to explore Da Lat. On weekends or major holidays, this place attracts a lot of tourists, so you should arrive early to conveniently buy tickets and visit without having to wait long.

2. Directions to Robin Hill Da Lat

Getting to Da Lat Robin Hill is quite simple, even for first-time visitors to the city of flowers. You can start from the Da Lat market area and follow the following route:

-

From Da Lat market, go through Ong Dao bridge, then turn left onto Tran Quoc Toan street.

-

Continue running straight for about 400m, you will come to a fifth intersection, please go straight onto Ho Tung Mau street.

-

At the end of Ho Tung Mau street, you will connect to Ba Thang April street - the main road leading to Prenn pass.

-

Near the end of Third Street April, you will see signboard to Robin Hill, turn in the direction to get there.

The total route only takes about 10–15 minutes by motorbike or car from the city center. For added convenience, you can use Google Maps to accurately locate and avoid getting lost. This is an easy route to follow and can be combined with visiting many famous nearby places such as Truc Lam Zen Monastery or Tuyen Lam Lake.

3. Robin Hill opening hours and latest Da Lat cable car ticket prices

Da Lat Robin Hill welcomes visitors every day from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.. During this period, the Da Lat cable car system also operates continuously, ready to serve tourists to experience the journey of flying through the beautiful pine forest.

To have a complete visit, you shouldarrange your schedule accordinglyto avoid arriving too early or too late compared to opening time. This not only helps you enjoy all the beauty of the area, but also avoids having to wait long or miss the opportunity to see the view from above.

Da Lat cable car ticket price list (from Robin Hill to Truc Lam Zen Monastery):

-

Adult:80,000 VND / round-trip ticket

-

Children:50,000 VND/round-trip ticket or 40,000 VND/one-way ticket

The above price is reasonable for a unique experience, where you can see the dreamy panoramic view of Da Lat from a height of 1,517m in mid-air, through the vast pine forest.

4. Experience conquering Robin Hill in Da Lat

4.1 The ideal time to explore Robin Hill in Da Lat

Photo: Robin Hill Dalat

If you are planning to visit Da Lat Robin Hill, the time frame from 9am to 11am is the perfect choice. At this time, Da Lat's typical cold weather combined with light morning sunshine creates a fresh, pleasant scene. In particular, sunlight that is not too harsh will help prevent glare from the cable car cabin, providing a complete sightseeing experience amidst cool green nature.

Besides, if you are passionate about "virtual living" and want to own sparkling photos, don't miss the time from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. **. At this time, the sunset sunlight gently penetrates the pine forest, reflecting on the cabins, creating a very artistic light effect. The poetic scenery and gentle afternoon sky will be the ideal backdrop for you to capture memorable moments while viewing the panoramic view of Da Lat city from above.

4.2 What to prepare when conquering Robin Hill in Da Lat?

Photo: Robin Hill Dalat

In order for the journey to explore Da Lat Robin Hill to go smoothly and completely, you should prepare some necessary items before departure. Here are the must-haves:

-

Lightweight clothing: Prioritize comfortable, easy-to-move-in clothes, combined with sports shoes or climbing shoes for ease of movement on steep hilly terrain.

-

Accessories included: Hat, jacket, scarf or scarf will help keep you warm and protected from the sun and wind.

-

Snacks and drinks: Helps you keep your energy up, especially if you plan to participate in many activities or travel by road.

-

Electronic devices: Phones, cameras, gimbals... are indispensable items to capture memorable moments amid the poetic natural scenery at Robin Hill.

A careful preparation will help you fully enjoy the fresh air and majestic beauty that this place brings.

4.3 Experience exciting activities at Robin Hill

Photo: Robin Hill Dalat

#### 4.3.1 Cloud hunting on top of Robin Hill – An experience not to be missed

One of the most popular activities when coming to Robin Hill is cloud hunting in the early morning. With an ideal height and wide open space, Robin Hill is a beautiful sunrise and cloud hunting spot, where you can admire the magical natural picture with layers of clouds floating lazily among the vast pine forest.

The ideal time to hunt clouds here is from 5am to 6am, when the sun begins to rise and the first sunlight shines through thin mist. However, you should pay attention to checking the weather forecast before going, because on days with thick fog, your ability to hunt clouds will be limited.

This is definitely a memorable experience for those who love nature and are passionate about exploring a dreamy Da Lat in the sunrise.

#### 4.3.2 Experience the Robin Hill cable car – A breathtaking aerial journey

After chasing clouds in the morning, don't miss the opportunity to experience the first and oldest cable car in Da Lat - a journey connecting Robin Hill with Truc Lam Zen Monastery.

With only about 12 minutes sitting on the cable car cabin, you will have the opportunity to admire the panoramic view of Da Lat city in the mist, the vast green pine forest and the romantic Tuyen Lam lake winding below. This is an unforgettable experience when you are suspended in mid-air, enjoying the fresh air and admiring the poetic beauty of the plateau.

The final destination is Truc Lam Zen Monastery– one of the most famous and sacred temples of Da Lat. The cable car journey is not simply about transportation, but also an emotional trip amidst the green and quiet nature of the land of flowers.

5. Notes to know when visiting Robin Hill in Da Lat

To make your journey to explore Da Lat Robin Hill go smoothly and memorable, please keep in mind a few important notes:

-

Restrict travel on holidays and weekends: This is the time when Da Lat welcomes a large number of visitors, easily leading to crowding and long queues to buy cable car tickets.

-

Prepare enough cash:Although many places support card payments, bringing cash is still a safe choice to buy tickets and make small purchases.

-

Follow the regulations when using the cable car: Please line up orderly, do not jostle or push to ensure the safety of yourself and everyone around you.

-

Buy food and drinks if needed: In the cable car area there are small convenience stores, you can easily buy more snacks if you forget to prepare in advance.

-

Capture memorable moments:Don't forget to check-in and save beautiful photos at Robin Hill - which offers a beautiful view of Da Lat from above.
