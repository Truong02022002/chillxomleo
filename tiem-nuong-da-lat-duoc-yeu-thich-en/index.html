*Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop*– Da Lat Grill Shop is famous not only for its cool climate and poetic scenery of the city of flowers but also for being an attractive culinary paradise for those who love grilled food. In the heart of Da Lat, Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop*** has quickly become a familiar destination for locals as well as tourists because of the harmonious combination of delicious food, Da Lat-style chill atmosphere and dedicated, professional service style.

1. Grilled Beef with Salted Egg Cheese – A popular specialty at Da Lat grill shop – Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

This is one of the most popular dishes at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop. Thick pieces of beef, grilled to perfection to retain the natural sweetness of the meat, are covered with a layer of fatty cheese and fragrant salted egg sauce. When enjoying, you will feel the delicate blend between the soft sweetness of beef and the characteristic salty fatty taste of salted egg cheese, creating an unforgettable culinary experience. This dish is very suitable for gatherings of friends or family on the chilly evenings of Da Lat.

2. Beef Belly Rolled with Kim Cham - attractive grilled dish at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop, Da Lat grill shop famous for its perfect blend of meat and vegetables.

The perfect combination of soft, rich beef brisket and sweet, crispy enoki mushrooms has created the unique enoki rolled beef belly dish at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop. When grilled over charcoal, the aroma of meat and mushrooms spreads, making it difficult for anyone to resist. This is also a great choice for those who love the balance between meat and vegetables, keeping fit while enjoying delicious food.

3. Grilled Beef Belly with Salt and Pepper – Attractive dish with traditional flavors at Da Lat grill shop – Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

For traditional food lovers, grilled beef belly with salt and pepper is a dish not to be missed. The beef is carefully marinated with salt and pepper, and when grilled over charcoal, it gives off a seductive aroma and a salty, mildly spicy taste that is very satisfying. This dish is very suitable for warm evenings at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop, helping you enjoy traditional flavors amidst the typical chill atmosphere of Da Lat.

4. Stuffed Snails – Traditional dish in a chill atmosphere at one of Da Lat's favorite grill shops

Stuffed snails are one of the traditional dishes rich in Vietnamese flavor that are meticulously prepared at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop. The snails are selected fresh, stuffed with meat to taste, then thoroughly steamed and dipped in fragrant ginger fish sauce. The delicious, rich flavor of the dish will definitely make you remember it forever.

5. Thai Spicy Ribs - New flavor for a warm evening at Da Lat grill shop

Inspired by the cuisine of the Golden Temple, Thai spicy ribs at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop brings a new experience with its characteristic spicy taste. The ribs are richly marinated, grilled to perfection, served with raw vegetables and sweet and sour Thai sauce, creating harmony in each bite. This is a dish that many diners remember forever after leaving Da Lat.

6. Shaken Sturgeon – Unforgettable, rich and delicious specialty at Da Lat grill shop – Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

If you want a change of taste after grilled meat dishes, shaking sturgeon at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop is the perfect choice. The piece of sturgeon is crispy on the outside, soft and sweet on the inside, covered with a rich, fatty and salty sauce. The dish brings newness and richness, very suitable for a cozy dinner in Da Lat.

7. Salt Roasted Sturgeon – A special delicious dish at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop (Da Lat grill shop)

Salt-roasted sturgeon is a dish not to be missed when coming to Da Lat grilled restaurant Tiem Xom Leo Grill & Chill. The fish is fried to a crispy golden brown, crispy on the outside, soft and sweet on the inside, combined with a layer of fragrant, lightly spicy lemongrass and garlic roasted salt to create an irresistible flavor. This is the ideal choice for those who want a change of taste from traditional grilled dishes. In the typical chill atmosphere of Da Lat, enjoying salt-roasted sturgeon with friends will definitely be a memorable experience.

8. Salted Egg Fried Shrimp - "Addictive" dish at Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

Fried shrimp with salted egg is a dish you should not miss when visiting Tiem Xom Leo Grill & Chill - Da Lat grill shop. Fresh shrimp are fried to a golden crisp, covered with a rich, fragrant layer of salted egg sauce, mixed with a little spicy and characteristic salty taste, creating a flavor that is both strange and attractive. This is the perfect choice to refresh your taste buds between rich grilled dishes. In the cold and chill atmosphere of Da Lat, sipping fried shrimp with salted egg with friends will be an interesting and memorable culinary experience.
