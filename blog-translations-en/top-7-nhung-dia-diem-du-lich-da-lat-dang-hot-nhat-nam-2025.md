# Top 7 hottest tourist destinations in Da Lat in 2025




![Top 7 hottest tourist destinations in Da Lat in 2025]]

Photo: Top 7 hottest tourist destinations in Da Lat in 2025





**Da Lat** is always the ideal destination for those who love romance, peace but no less exciting. If you are planning a **trip to Dalat**, let's explore the places that are making waves in the **city of flowers** this year that you should not miss!

#### 🌟 **Kombi Land Coffee - Unique Check-in Point When Traveling to Dalat**

![Kombi Land Coffee - Unique Check-in Point When Traveling to Da Lat]]

Photo: Kombi Land Coffee - Unique Check-in Point When Traveling to Dalat

Standing out with its wild and unique design style, Kombi Land Coffee is likened to a "little desert in the heart of Da Lat". This is one of the indispensable cafes in the list of attractions when **traveling to Dalat**.

**Special point:**

✅ Cactus trees, antique cars and lakes are highlights that attract tourists.
✅ The shop also organizes musical activities in the evening, bringing an interesting experience.

📍 **Address:** Mimosa Pass, Ward 10, Da Lat

#### 🌈 **Green Hill - Virtual Paradise When Traveling to Da Lat**

As an emerging location but has quickly captured the hearts of young people, **Green Chou Hill** is the ideal place for you to immerse yourself in nature and enjoy the peaceful space of **Da Lat Tourism**.

**Attractive points:**

🌅 The reed grass field is brightly colored when dusk falls.
📸 Vintage style decorated areas reserved for lifelong photos.

📍 **Address:** Near Suoi Vang Tourist Area, Da Lat

#### 🌳 **Dalaland Coffee – Miniature Bali in the Middle of Da Lat Tourism**

![Da Lat Travel - Dalaland Coffee]]

Photo: Da Lat Tourism - Dalaland Coffee

Dalaland Coffee is known as a "miniature Bali" version in the heart of Da Lat. If you want to find a place to enjoy coffee and a relaxing space during your **Da Lat trip**, this is the perfect choice.

**Highlights:**

🌴 The space has a tropical breath, suitable for relaxing and taking photos.
🍰 Enjoy coffee and cakes in a romantic space.

**📍 Address:** Village 2, Ta Nung Commune, Da Lat

#### 🌄 **Da Phu Hill Rattan Hunting Wooden Bridge - Unique Experience When Traveling to Da Lat**

One of the activities not to be missed when **traveling to Da Lat** is cloud hunting in the early morning. Da Phu Hill Cloud Hunting Wooden Bridge will give you the opportunity to see the picturesque sea of ​​floating clouds.

**Interesting point:**

Majestic scene with white clouds drifting lazily.

Quiet space, very suitable for meditation or relaxation.

**⏰ Ideal time:** From 5am - 7am ​​to have the opportunity to admire the most beautiful sea of ​​clouds.

**📍 Address:** Da Phu Hill, Ward 7, Da Lat

#### 🍃 **Chill Grill Shop Xom Leo - Dining Destination You Can't Miss When Traveling to Da Lat**

Not only the attractions, **Da Lat tourism** is also attractive by its unique cuisine. If you are a fan of grilled dishes, then  [**Neighbourhood Grill & Chill Shop**] is a place you must definitely visit!

🔥 **Features:**

🥩 Diverse menu with fragrant grilled dishes.
🍷 Warm space, suitable for gathering friends and family.

**Detailed information:**

📍 Address: 113 Huynh Tan Phat, Ward 11, Da Lat, Lam Dong 67000

📩 Inbox: [https://m.me/nuongxomleo]

#### 🌿 **Green Hills - Green Hills In Dalat Travel Itinerary**

If you love nature and want to experience the feeling of camping in the middle of a pine forest when **traveling to Da Lat**, Green Hills is the ideal destination.

**Special point:**

🏕️ Large space, suitable for team building activities.
🌄 The view from the hill overlooking the valley is very beautiful, especially in the afternoon.

📍 **Address:** Near Datanla Waterfall, Da Lat

## 🏞️ **Lac Tien Gioi Tourist Area - Fairy Tale Space When Traveling to Dalat**

If you want to find a magical and poetic space during your trip to Dalat, then Lac Tien Gioi Tourist Area is definitely an option not to be missed.

🎹 **Highlights:**

🚣 Clear blue lake with swan boats.

🎼 The pianos on the lake are a check-in spot not to be missed.

**📍 Address:** Ankroet Street, Da Lat

**Da Lat tourism** is always attractive with new and unique places, from majestic nature to modern relaxing spaces. If you are planning to explore **Da Lat**, don't forget to visit the hot places we just suggested.

👉 And especially, come to **Grill & Chill Shop in Hamlet Leo** to enjoy authentic Da Lat dishes in a cozy space.
