# Top 7 hottest tourist destinations in Da Lat in 2025

**Da Lat** is always the ideal destination for those who love romance, peace but no less exciting. If you are planning a **trip to Da Lat**, let's explore the places that are making waves in the **city of flowers** this year that you should not miss!

#### 🌟**Kombi Land Coffee - Unique Check-in Point When Traveling to Da Lat**

Standing out with its wild and unique design style, Kombi Land Coffee is likened to a "little desert in the heart of Da Lat". This is one of the indispensable cafes in the list of attractions when traveling to Da Lat.

**Special point:**

✅ Cactus trees, antique cars and lakes are highlights that attract tourists.
✅ The shop also organizes musical activities in the evening, bringing an interesting experience.

📍**Address:**Mimosa Pass, Ward 10, Da Lat

#### 🌈**Green-Colored Chou Hill - Virtual Living Paradise When Traveling to Da Lat**

As a newly emerging location but has quickly captured the hearts of young people, **Green Chou Hill** is the ideal place for you to immerse yourself in nature and enjoy the peaceful space of **Da Lat Tourism**.

**Attractive points:**

🌅 The reed grass field is brightly colored when dusk falls.
📸 Vintage style decorated areas reserved for lifelong photos.

📍**Address:**Near Suoi Vang Tourist Area, Da Lat

#### 🌳**Dalaland Coffee – Miniature Bali in the Middle of Da Lat Tourism**

Dalaland Coffee is known as a "miniature Bali" version in the heart of Da Lat. If you want to find a place to enjoy coffee and relax during your trip to Da Lat, this is the perfect choice.

**Highlights:**

🌴 The space has a tropical breath, suitable for relaxing and taking photos.
🍰 Enjoy coffee and cakes in a romantic space.

**📍 Address:**Village 2, Ta Nung Commune, Da Lat

#### 🌄**Wooden Bridge to Hunt Rattan at Da Phu Hill - Unique Experience When Traveling to Da Lat**

One of the activities not to be missed when traveling to Da Lat is cloud hunting in the early morning. Da Phu Hill Cloud Hunting Wooden Bridge will give you the opportunity to see the picturesque sea of ​​floating clouds.

**Interesting point:**

Majestic scene with white clouds drifting lazily.

Quiet space, very suitable for meditation or relaxation.

**⏰ Ideal time: **From 5am - 7am ​​to have the opportunity to admire the most beautiful sea of ​​clouds.

**📍 Address:**Da Phu Hill, Ward 7, Da Lat

#### 🌿**Green Hills - Green Hills In Da Lat Travel Itinerary**

If you love nature and want to experience the feeling of camping in the middle of a pine forest when **traveling to Da Lat**, Green Hills is the ideal destination.

**Special point:**

🏕️ Large space, suitable for team building activities.
🌄 The view from the hill overlooking the valley is very beautiful, especially in the afternoon.

📍**Address:**Near Datanla Waterfall, Da Lat

## 🏞️**Lac Tien Gioi Tourist Area - Fairy Tale Space When Traveling to Da Lat**

If you want to find a magical and poetic space during your trip to Da Lat, then Lac Tien Gioi Tourist Area is definitely an option not to be missed.

🎹**Highlights:**

🚣 Clear blue lake with swan boats.

🎼 The pianos on the lake are a check-in spot not to be missed.

**📍 Address:**Ankroet Street, Da Lat
