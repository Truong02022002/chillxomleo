# Da Lat at Night - Top Must-Visit Places




![Da Lat at Night - Top Must-Visit Places]]

Photo: Da Lat at Night - Top Must-Visit Places







Da Lat not only attracts tourists with its cool climate and poetic landscape during the day but is also very charming when night falls. Coming to the paved streets, bustling night markets, and shimmering cafes have created a unique Da Lat nightlife that cannot be missed. Join us to discover the top places to visit when coming to Da Lat at night!

![Dalat]]

Photo: Da Lat

### 1. 🌟 Dalat Night Market - Food Paradise

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

Dalat Night Market is a destination not to be missed when you want to feel the refreshing breath of the city of flowers. Here, you will find a series of delicious specialties such as grilled rice paper, wormwood, shaking beef, mulberry juice...

**👉 Suggestion:** Visit the grilled rice paper shops right in the center of the night market to enjoy the great taste!

### 2. 🔥 Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop – Train Hunting at Night to See the Cage House

![Dalat]]

Photo: Da Lat

If you want to chill and enjoy delicious grilled dishes, Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop is the ideal choice. This place is famous for its cozy space, ideal for night train hunting and watching the brightly lit cage houses in the valley.

Every evening, from Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop, you can capture the moment when the night train passes through the middle of the valley, creating a romantic and nostalgic scene. There's nothing better than sipping hot grilled dishes while watching the bright lantern lights reflect on the foggy landscape.

**🌟 Best Seller:**

- **Bon Tang with Salted Egg Cheese Sauce** – Tender beef, blended with an attractive, fatty sauce.

- **Thai Spicy Ribs** – Rich, spicy, conquers right from the first bite.

- **Stuffed Snails** – Typical dish, delicious and attractive.

- **Korean Pork Belly** – Richly marinated, grilled crispy, served with authentic Korean kimchi.

**👉 Suggestion:** Come early to choose the spot with the best view and enjoy the sparkling space at night! Don't forget to bring your camera to capture beautiful moments when the night train passes by!

### 3. 🌌 Camping on LangBiang Peak

![Dalat]]

Photo: Da Lat

If you want a challenging and romantic experience, try camping on LangBiang peak. The feeling of looking at the city from above, the cold air and the starry sky will be a memorable experience.

**👉 Suggestion:** Remember to bring warm clothes because the night temperature is very cold!

### 4. 🥛 Fatty Hot Milk - Authentic Da Lat Flavor

![Dalat]]

Photo: Da Lat

One of the experiences not to be missed when coming to Da Lat at night is enjoying a glass of hot milk at **Dung Beo Hot Milk** shop. This is a familiar address for locals and tourists because of its rich, delicious milk flavor.

Not only hot milk, the shop also serves many delicious side dishes such as pepper cakes, salt and chili toast or grilled rice paper. The cozy space and the mist of Da Lat at night will make you feel relaxed and peaceful.

**👉 Suggestion:** Try hot soy milk or milk cocoa to feel the unique flavor of the shop!

🌟 **Da Lat at night** is truly different, sparkling and magical. Among them, Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop is a place not to be missed. Not only does it bring an attractive culinary experience with delicious grilled dishes, but this place also gives you the opportunity to hunt for trains at night, watching the bright lantern lights in a romantic chill space. If you are looking for an ideal place to enjoy Da Lat at night, don't forget to visit Tiem Grill & Chill Xom Leo!
