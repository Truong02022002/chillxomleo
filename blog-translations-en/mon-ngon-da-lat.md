After a day of exploring Da Lat with its fresh nature and cool air, evening is the ideal time for you to relax at the dining table, enjoying the typical culinary flavors of the mountain town. And if you are wondering where to have dinner among countless options, then [**Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop**] is a meeting place not to be missed.

Nestled in the middle of the pine forest, **Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop** is one of the **Da Lat grill shops** with the most "expensive" view space**. From the shop, you can **see the entire sunset**, **the train weaving through the forest**, and **Da Lat's cage house glowing at night** - all creating an unforgettable poetic scene.
