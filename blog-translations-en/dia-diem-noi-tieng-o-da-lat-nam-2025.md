# Famous places in Da Lat in 2025

Da Lat - the city of dreams with a cool climate all year round, beautiful natural scenery and peaceful atmosphere. This is the ideal destination for those who want to enjoy a relaxing vacation or explore unique destinations. Below is a list of famous places in Da Lat that you cannot miss.

### 1.**Xuan Huong Lake - Symbol of Da Lat**

🌊**Outstanding features:**
Xuan Huong Lake is located right in the city center, a familiar destination for both tourists and locals. With clear blue water, surrounding pine trees and fresh air, this is the ideal place to stroll, cycle or simply sit and watch the sunset.

🍵**Interesting activities:**

Enjoy morning coffee at lakeside shops.

Cycling around the lake to feel the peaceful beauty.

Visit the nearby city flower garden to take photos and admire thousands of colorful flowers.

### 2.**Valley of Love – Romantic destination**

💖**Special features:**
Located about 5 km from the city center, Valley of Love is a convergence of poetic natural landscapes with lakes, pine forests and unique man-made structures.

📸**Ideal for:**

Couples want to keep memorable photos.

Go for a walk, row a boat on the lake or try team building games.

### 3.**Bao Dai Palace – Ancient architecture**

🏛️**Introduction:**
Bao Dai Palace is the residence and working place of King Bao Dai - the last king of the Nguyen Dynasty. With French design combined with Vietnamese architecture, this mansion brings a feeling of both ancient and elegant.

🎨**Experience:**

Visit the king's offices, bedrooms and living rooms.

Take photos in royal costumes to feel the royal atmosphere.

### 4.**Da Lat Station – The oldest train station in Vietnam**

🚂**Special feature:**
Da Lat Station was built during the French colonial period and is one of the oldest stations in Indochina. With a unique design and nostalgic space, this is a favorite place for those who are passionate about photography.

🌄**Activities:**

Experience the train route to Trai Mat, admiring the peaceful scenery.

Take photos at the ancient train carriages and station area.

### 5.**Da Lat Night Market – Food Paradise**

🌟**Destination not to be missed:**
Da Lat Night Market is where visitors can enjoy delicious food and explore nightlife in the dreamy city.

🍢**Outstanding delicacies:**

Grilled rice paper, hot soy milk.

Avocado cream, grilled spring rolls.

Dried fruits and Da Lat specialties.

🛍️**Note:**
Don't forget to buy some souvenirs or specialties such as jam or artichoke tea as gifts.

### 6.**Clay Tunnel – Unique project**

🏺**Description:**
Clay Tunnel is located in the middle of a vast pine forest, recreating Da Lat's history and culture through sculptures made of red basalt clay. This is an interesting destination to learn about the land and people here.

🎯**Highlight:**

The works of art simulate the scenery of Da Lat.

Infinity Lake - a famous check-in place with two giant statues.

### 7.**Cau Dat Tea Plateau – Green beauty**

🌿**Features:**
About 25 km from the city center, Cau Dat tea hill is an ideal place to immerse yourself in nature. With vast tea hills and cool air, this is the perfect destination for "virtual living" believers.

📷**Activities:**

Check-in with a view of lush green tea hills.

Enjoy tea at a small cafe located in the middle of a tea hill.

### 9.**Tuyen Lam Lake – Quiet space**

🌅**Description:**
Tuyen Lam Lake is the largest freshwater lake in Da Lat, located between hills and green pine forests. This is the ideal place to enjoy fresh air and participate in interesting activities.

🛶**Activities:**

Kayak or paddleboard.

Camp by the lake to enjoy the romantic sunset.

### 10.**Cu Lan Village – Peaceful village space**

🌳**Destination:**
Cu Lan Village is a small village located in the middle of a pine forest, outstanding with its pristine natural landscape and interesting experiential activities.

🛤️**Activities:**

Learn about local culture.

Try games like rowing and off-road racing.

Da Lat is a convergence of natural scenery, rich culture and cuisine. Whether it's your first time or you've visited many times, this city always offers exciting and new experiences.
