# Da Lat Destination Review – Latest Spots in 2025

![Da Lat Destination Review – Latest Spots in 2025]

Photo: Da Lat Destination Review – Latest Spots in 2025

Da Lat, with its romantic charm and fresh highland air, is an unmissable destination for travel lovers. This dreamy city never fails to surprise visitors with new and unique experiences. Below is a detailed review of the latest must-visit spots in Da Lat to inspire your next adventure.

### 1. **Discover New Destinations in Da Lat**

#### **1.1. Thien Phuc Duc Hill**

![Da Lat]

Photo: Da Lat

🌄 **Standout feature:** A favorite spot for photographers, famous for its lone pine tree and breathtaking cloud-hunting views at dawn.

📸 **Activities:** Photography and overnight camping to witness a stunning sunrise.

#### **1.2. Infinity Lake (Hồ Vô Cực)**

![Da Lat]

Photo: Da Lat

🌊 **What makes it special:** One of the hottest new check-in spots, featuring a massive two-faced sculpture overlooking the lake.

🌺 **Ideal for:** Couples or friend groups looking to capture artistic, Instagrammable photos.

#### **1.3. La Phong Tourist Area**

![Da Lat]

Photo: Da Lat

🌿 **What makes it unique:** The grounds are designed with Japanese-inspired aesthetics, including red maple forests, crystal-clear streams, and distinctive art installations.

**Suggestion:** Take a leisurely walk or relax at one of the cafés within the complex.

### 2. **Fun & Unique Experiences**

#### **2.1. Cloud Hunting on Langbiang Peak**

![Da Lat]

Photo: Da Lat

🏔️ **Why you should try it:** Langbiang Peak is famous not only for its majestic natural scenery but also as the ultimate spot for cloud hunting at dawn.

🚵 **Other activities:** Mountain trekking, jeep rides, and exploring the culture of the K'ho ethnic group.

#### **2.2. Strawberry Picking Tours**

![Da Lat]

Photo: Da Lat

🍓 **Where to go:** Large strawberry farms in Da Lat such as Hiep Luc or Biofresh.

🎯 **The experience:** Visitors get to pick and taste sun-ripened strawberries straight from the garden.

#### **2.3. Explore Quirky Cafés**

☕ **The Married Beans:** A warm, cozy space serving premium-quality coffee.

🌉 **Kokoro Café:** A Japanese-inspired café featuring a beautiful koi pond and picture-perfect photo corners.

### 4. **Tips for Traveling in Da Lat**

👕 **Clothing:** Bring a jacket and comfortable shoes, as the weather gets quite cold, especially in the early morning and at night.

🛵 **Rent a motorbike:** For convenient exploration, renting a motorbike is the best option at very affordable rates.

🌿 **Be eco-friendly:** Please don't leave trash at tourist sites — help preserve Da Lat's beautiful natural landscapes.

Da Lat always finds ways to reinvent itself with exciting new destinations and experiences. We hope this guide inspires you for your upcoming trip.
