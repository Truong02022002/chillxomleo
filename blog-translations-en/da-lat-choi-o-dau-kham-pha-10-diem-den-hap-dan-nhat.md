# Where to play in Da Lat? Explore the 10 most attractive destinations of 2025

**Da Lat** - the city of flowers in the middle of the Lam Vien plateau, where fog covers the path and cold winds creep through each row of pine trees, has long been the dream destination of millions of tourists. Dreamy beauty, mild climate all year round and poetic scenery have made this place an irresistible attraction. Coming to Da Lat, you will not only be able to immerse yourself in fresh nature but also live slowly amid the increasingly hurried pace of modern life.

If you are planning an upcoming trip and still wondering **where to visit in Da Lat**, don't worry! This foggy city has countless attractive destinations to suit all tastes - from magnificent natural landscapes, ancient architecture, to unique culinary and art spaces. Let's explore the 10 extremely hot locations below to have a memorable and complete journey in this dreamy city!

## **1. Where to play first in Da Lat? Let's start at Xuan Huong Lake - The heart of the city**

If anyone asks where is the "heart of Da Lat", they certainly cannot help but mention **Xuan Huong Lake**. Located right in the city center, the lake possesses a gentle, poetic beauty with a calm water surface surrounded by green pine rows and smooth grass.

Here, you can choose from many interesting activities:

-
Cycling around the lake, enjoying the fresh air.

-
Swan boating - a romantic experience for couples.

-
Stop to enjoy a cup of hot coffee at lakeside shops with beautiful views.

Xuan Huong Lake is the sweet and memorable first greeting that Da Lat sends to visitors.

## **3. Where to go in Da Lat to enjoy nature? Cau Dat Tea Hill – Endless green color**

About 20km from the center of Da Lat, Cau Dat tea hill is a favorite place for those who are passionate about photography and want to immerse themselves in nature. The tea beds stretch out, covering the entire hillside with green, creating an extremely relaxing scene.

-
The best time is early in the morning when the dew is still light and the sun is just coming out.

-
You can bring a camera or hire a professional photography service to capture wonderful moments here.

-
There is also Cau Dat Farm coffee shop with a view of the entire tea valley.

## **4. Where to hang out in Da Lat for couples? Valley of Love – Romantic rendezvous**

For a long time, **Valley of Love** has become a symbol of romance in Da Lat. This place possesses a charming scenery with green pine hills, clear lakes and hundreds of blooming flowers.

-
Rowing a swan boat in the middle of the lake.

-
Walk through miniature landscapes such as the heart gate, love lock bridge, and flower tunnel.

-
Taking wedding or couple photos is extremely "artistic".

With spacious space and professional travel services, Valley of Love is a place not to be missed for those who are in love or simply want to find peace.

## **5. Where to hang out in Da Lat at night? Don't miss Da Lat Night Market - Food paradise**

When night falls, Da Lat city puts on a different, sparkling look. **Da Lat Night Market** is a crowded and bustling gathering place but still retains its unique charm.

-
Famous snacks such as grilled rice paper, skewers, grilled corn with onions, grilled sweet potatoes,...

-
Typical drinks include hot soy milk and ginger tea.

-
Many stalls sell woolen items, souvenirs, and local specialties at reasonable prices.

The night market is a great place to both enjoy delicious food and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Da Lat at night.

## **6. Where to visit in Da Lat to admire French architecture? Chicken Church – Ancient symbol**

Da Lat Cathedral, often called **Chicken Cathedral**, is a classic European-style architectural work, built during the French colonial period. The reason for its name is because at the top of the bell tower there is a statue of a large chicken - a symbol of alertness.

-
This place not only attracts tourists but is also a spiritual destination for many local people.

-
The orange-pink wall, arched door frame and ancient garden are ideal for taking photos in a classic, vintage style.

## **7. Where to play in Da Lat if you love nostalgia? Da Lat Station – The most ancient station in Vietnam**

**Da Lat Station** is one of the remaining structures from the French period and is considered the oldest station in Vietnam. With a pyramidal tile roof, characteristic yellow paint and ancient train carriages, this place is an indispensable check-in point for those who love photography.

-
Visitors can buy tickets to experience the short train ride from Da Lat Station to Trai Mat - passing through poetic villages and pine forests.

-
There are also many vintage cafes around the station, very suitable for resting.

## **8. Where to play in Da Lat to immerse yourself in nature? Cu Lan Village – Peace in the middle of the mountains and forests**

Hidden deep in the middle of the pine forest, Cu Lan Village is a small village with wild beauty, where visitors can truly "escape" the city to enjoy nature.

-
Kayaking on the stream.

-
Ride a jeep to explore pine forests and rocky streams.

-
Participate in picnics, camping, and teambuilding activities.

Not only a destination for those who love nature, Cu Lan Village is also suitable for groups of friends and families who want to experience something new and close.

## **9. Where to visit in Da Lat to experience adventure? Datanla Waterfall – Attraction from wild nature**

Da Lat is famous for many beautiful waterfalls, but if talking about adventure activities, Datanla waterfall is the top name. Located only about 5km from the center, this place is a stop for those who want to "exceed their limits".

-
Sliding slide – addictive game, suitable for all ages.

-
Climbing the waterfall - for adventurous people.

-
Cable car passes through the pine forest, viewing the majestic panorama of nature.

### **10. Where to visit in Da Lat to explore art? Clay tunnel – Wonders from human hands**

Completely different from traditional tourist attractions, the clay tunnel is a giant work of art sculpted entirely from basalt clay. Along the more than 1km long road are models reproducing Da Lat's history, Central Highlands culture and many modern symbols.

-
A special highlight is **Infinite Lake** - a place known as the "virtual living sanctuary" with views of mountains and forests and a reflective lake surface.

-
The space is suitable for families, groups of friends, and couples.
