# Explore Da Lat - City of Fog

## Introduction to Da Lat - City of Thousand Flowers

Da Lat - the most famous tourist destination in Vietnam, is known for its cold climate all year round, vast green pine hills and countless colorful flowers blooming in all four seasons. Located on the Lam Vien plateau, this place is not only an ideal resort paradise with a peaceful, fresh space but also an attractive destination thanks to its poetic landscapes, ancient architecture, unique culture and rich cuisine, captivating tourists from near and far.

## Attractive Tourist Destinations In Da Lat

### 1. Xuan Huong Lake - The Heart of Da Lat

Xuan Huong Lake is the symbol of the city, where visitors can enjoy fresh air, take a leisurely walk or row a boat.

### 2. Valley of Love – Romantic Destination

Located about 5km from the center, this place possesses beautiful natural landscapes, very suitable for couples.

### 3. Cau Dat Tea Hill – Check-in for Extreme Virtual Living

If you are a fan of virtual living, Cau Dat Tea Hill with its lush green tea beds will definitely be a destination not to be missed.

### 4. Da Lat Night Market - Food Paradise

At the night market, you can enjoy many delicious dishes such as grilled rice paper, soy milk, grilled sweet potatoes,...

## Da Lat Cuisine - Rich Taste of the Highlands

### 1. Grilled Rice Paper – “Vietnamese Pizza”

A famous snack, combining crispy grilled rice paper, fatty eggs, melted cheese and many attractive toppings such as sausage, fried onions, shrimp, and mayonnaise.

### 2. Hot Soy Milk – An Indispensable Drink

A glass of hot soy milk on a cold evening not only gives you a warm feeling but also helps relax and soothe the body.
