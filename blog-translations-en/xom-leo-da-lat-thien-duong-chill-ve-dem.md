# Explore the peaceful chill corner at Xom Leo village in Da Lat at night

Da Lat is inherently famous for its dreamy beauty and cold, romantic atmosphere. But if you want to find a quiet, artistic and poetic private corner, **Xom Leo Da Lat Village at night** is the ideal place to relax and recharge. Not only is it a stopover for souls who love rusticity, this place is also a cool "chill location" with sparkling lights, nostalgic style and attractive local culinary flavors.

## 1. Where is Xom Leo Village located in Da Lat?

Xom Leo area is located in a quite quiet location, about 5km from the city center. Although located outside the crowded area, this place has the simple beauty of the suburbs - with small alleys, old wooden houses, and cafes typical of old Da Lat. It feels like time slows down, allowing you to live slowly and fully enjoy each moment.

## 2. What's special about Xom Leo Da Lat at night?

Leo village in Da Lat at night
### Romantic space with magical lights

When night falls, the whole village seems to put on a new coat - shimmering yellow lights from small shops, mixed with dim light from the fog. With just a cup of hot cocoa or a glass of warm soy milk, you can immerse yourself in the fresh air and quietly watch the city from afar sparkling like the Milky Way.

### Classic style – Extremely artistic virtual living corner

From pastel-painted walls, old wooden tables and chairs to retro signs, every corner here becomes the perfect backdrop for "chill" photos. Whether you are an art lover or simply like a quiet feeling, this place will definitely fascinate you.
