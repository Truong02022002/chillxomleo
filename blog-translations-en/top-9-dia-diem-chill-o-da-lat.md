# Top 9 chill places in Da Lat for a relaxing & virtual living experience




![🌟 Top 9 chill places in Da Lat for a relaxing & virtual living experience]]

Photo: 🌟 Top 9 chill places in Da Lat for a relaxing & virtual living experience







**Da Lat**, the city of flowers, is famous not only for its cool climate all year round but also for its true **chill** moments - where you can immerse yourself in nature, sip a cup of hot coffee in a romantic space or enjoy a BBQ dinner in the middle of the pine forest. If you are looking for beautiful **chill places in Da Lat** to "take each other away", let's explore the following 9 suggestions right away - from nature to the coolest **Da Lat chill cafes**, which will definitely make you want to come back again and again.

## 1. Chill amidst peaceful nature

### 🌊 Tuyen Lam Lake - Chill amidst quiet nature

Located about 7km from the city center, **Tuyen Lam Lake** is an ideal destination for those who want to get away from the noisy city. With a lake surface as calm as a mirror, surrounded by vast pine forests, this place is suitable for you to:

👉 Stroll along the lake

👉 Organize a picnic

👉 Rowing in a peaceful space

If you are looking for a **chill place in Da Lat near nature**, Tuyen Lam Lake is a great choice.

### 🍃 Cau Dat Tea Hill - Green space for relaxation & virtual living

**Cau Dat Tea Hill** is not only famous for its vast stretches of tea hills, but is also an ideal place for her to take "million-like" photos with a lush green background and clear sky.

Here, you can also:

👉 Enjoy coffee at Cau Dat Farm

👉 Watch the romantic sunset among the tea hills

👉 Chill with fresh air at an altitude of over 1,600m

### 🌲 Cu Lan Village - Chill spot between forests & streams

Located deep in the primeval forest, **Cu Lan Village** is one of the **little-known chill places in Dalat** but extremely worth experiencing.

Featured activities:

👉 Camping in the middle of pristine nature

👉 Chèo thuyền trên hồ

👉 Explore folk games and culture of the Central Highlands

The quiet space here is extremely suitable for those who want to "detox" their soul after busy days.

## 3. Chill out and experience cloud hunting & camping

## 🌥️ Cloud hunting in Dalat at Thien Phuc Duc Hill

We can't talk about chill without forgetting the **Da Lat cloud hunting experience**. And **Thien Phuc Duc hill** is an ideal place to hunt clouds every early morning.

You should go from 4:30 a.m. to 5 a.m. to catch:

👉 Sea of ​​floating white clouds

👉 Bright sunrise scene behind the pine forest

👉 Magical chill photos like fairy tales

This is also an ideal camping location for groups of friends or nature-loving couples.
