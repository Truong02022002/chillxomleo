# ✨ Explore Da Lat 2 days 1 night




![✨ Explore Da Lat 2 days 1 night]]

Photo: ✨ Explore Da Lat 2 days 1 night







Da Lat, the city of flowers, is always an attractive destination for those who love cool climate and poetic landscapes. If you only have 2 days and 1 night to explore, don't worry! With the following itinerary, you will have a complete and memorable trip.

### **🗓️ Day 1: Explore romantic Da Lat**

#### 🌄 **Morning: Good morning in the foggy city**

**⏰ 5:00 – 6:00**: Arrive in Da Lat
Start your journey with a night bus or early morning flight to be in Da Lat early in the morning. The cool air and mist covering everywhere will be the perfect greeting for you.

**🍜 6:30 – 7:30**: Have breakfast at a local restaurant
Enjoy a bowl of hot banh can, beef noodle soup or Quang noodles - typical breakfast dishes of Da Lat. Don't forget to sip a cup of hot milk coffee to energize.

**📍 8:00 – 11:00**: Visit famous places

**Tuyen Lam Lake**:

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

🛶 An ideal destination with poetic natural scenery, where you can kayak or walk around the lake.

**Truc Lam Zen Monastery**:

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

🏯 The monastery is located on a high hill, providing a peaceful space and beautiful views of Tuyen Lam Lake.

#### 🍽️ **Noon: Enjoy Dalat cuisine**

**⏰ 11:30 – 13:00**: Lunch at a local restaurant
Visit a restaurant to enjoy specialty dishes such as **grilled spring rolls**, or **grilled chicken and bamboo tube rice**. This is the time for you to recharge after a busy morning.

#### 🌅 **Afternoon: Check-in and explore more**

**📸 14:00 – 16:00**: Check-in at virtual destinations

**Lam Vien Square**:

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

🌼 Famous symbol with wild sunflowers and giant artichoke buds. This is an indispensable place in your photo album.

**City flower garden**: 🌸

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

A brilliant space with thousands of blooming flowers, a perfect stop for those who love nature.

**🏡 16:30 – 17:30**: Check in and rest
Choose a lovely hotel or homestay to enjoy a relaxing space. Da Lat has many options from luxurious to cozy, suitable for all needs.

#### 🌙 **Evening: Enjoy food and explore the night market**

**🔥 18:00 – 20:00**: Have dinner at **Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop**
Don't miss the opportunity to enjoy delicious grilled dishes at [**Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop**]. The cozy space and rich menu will bring you a memorable dinner.

**🎡 20:30 - 22:00**: Explore Da Lat night market
The night market is where you can enjoy street food such as grilled rice paper, soy milk, or grilled sweet potatoes. This is also an opportunity for you to shop for specialties and souvenirs.

### **🗓️ Day 2: Gentle experience before leaving Da Lat**

#### 🌞 **Morning: Watch the sunrise and relax**

**⏰ 5:30 – 7:30**: Watch the sunrise at **Da Phu Hill**

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat
If you are a lover of tranquility and nature, get up early to watch the sunrise at Da Phu Hill. The sunrise over the green pine hills will be a moment you cannot forget.

**☕ 8:00 – 9:30**: Enjoy breakfast and coffee

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat
Visit a cafe with a beautiful view like  **Panorama Cafe** to enjoy a light breakfast while enjoying the panoramic view of Da Lat in the morning light.

#### 🏰 **Noon: End the journey with destinations near the center**

**📍 10:00 – 11:30**: Visit destinations near the center

**Chicken Church**:

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

⛪ An ancient Gothic architectural work, symbol of Da Lat.

**Bao Dai Palace**:

![Da Lat]]

Photo: Da Lat

🏛️ Learn history and admire the classical architecture of the last king of the Nguyen Dynasty.

**🍜 12:00**: Have lunch and prepare to leave Da Lat
Enjoy a quick lunch of **beef noodle soup** or **shu mai banh mi** before getting back to your normal life.

### **🌟 Notes for a perfect trip**

- **🧳 Book tickets and rooms early**: Especially in peak season, bus tickets and hotel rooms often sell out quickly.

- **🧥 Bring warm clothes**: Whether it's summer or winter, Da Lat's weather is always cold, especially in the evening.

- **🕒 Prepare a flexible schedule**: To avoid missing out on beautiful moments, reserve time for outdoor activities.

Even with only 2 days and 1 night, Da Lat still brings you memorable experiences. From the poetic natural scenery, rich cuisine, to sparkling check-in spots, this short journey will help you relax and refresh yourself.

Don't forget to visit **🔥 Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop** to enjoy unique flavors and save beautiful memories with friends and family. **Da Lat always welcomes you with fresh air and wonderful experiences!**
