# Dalat Night Market - Food paradise in the heart of the city




![Dalat Night Market - Food paradise in the heart of the city]]

Photo: Da Lat Night Market - Food paradise in the heart of the city







***Da Lat Night Market***, also known by its popular name ***Am Phu Market***, is not only a famous sightseeing destination but also a place that clearly shows the daily life and typical culture of the people of the dreamy mountain town.

![Da Lat Night Market]]

Photo: Da Lat Night Market

If you want to feel the bustle and crowd of ***[Da Lat]*** when night falls, you should definitely visit the bustling night market right in the city center. Here, there are not only hundreds of stalls of all kinds, but also a culinary paradise that makes it difficult for visitors to resist.

## 1. Some details about Da Lat night market

***Da Lat Night Market*** also has the familiar nickname "***Am Phu Market***". This curious name originates from the time when the market was first formed. At that time, there were only a few street vendors selling food late at night, flickering under the light of oil lamps in the cold night mist - creating a dim space like the "underworld", from which the unique name was born.

![Da Lat Night Market]]

Photo: Da Lat Night Market

Nowadays, the night market has become a familiar destination every night. From around 5pm, stalls start to set up, being most vibrant around 7pm until late. This is the time when visitors can immerse themselves in the vibrant life of Da Lat, shopping and enjoying local specialties.

## 2. Where is Da Lat Night Market located & how to get there

- **Address:** Nguyen Thi Minh Khai Street, Ward 1, City. Da Lat

The night market is located right in the center of Da Lat, convenient for both residents and tourists to visit. If you stay in the central area or near Ward 2 and Ward 10, you can walk to the market. For those who live farther away, you can rent a motorbike (price from 100,000 - 170,000 VND/day), take a taxi or call a motorbike taxi, which are all very convenient.

Starting from Xuan Huong Lake, just cross a small bridge and a roundabout to see the bustling night market. The parking lot here includes both residential and public parking areas and is very easy to find.

**Suggestion:** Refer to the list of quality car companies going to Da Lat to plan your trip in advance.

## 4. What is attractive about Da Lat Night Market for shopping?

If you've been to Da Lat night market and haven't stopped by the clothing, agricultural products, souvenirs stalls... then you're missing out. Not only is it a place to enjoy street food, the market is also a shopping paradise for visitors with a variety of items at affordable prices, suitable to bring home as gifts or simply to reward yourself with a small, beautiful item.

### 4.1 Cheap second-hand clothes at Da Lat night market

A special feature at Da Lat night market are the stalls selling second-hand clothes, where you can find used but very new clothes at super economical prices. In addition, the market also has many new clothing stores with all styles - from dynamic and individual to gentle and vintage. This is truly a favorite destination for fashionistas who like to "hunt for unique items".

## 5. Experience going to Da Lat night market for first-time visitors

To make your journey to explore Da Lat night market more complete, especially for those who come here for the first time, you should refer to a few experiences below to prepare better!

### 5.1 Until what time is Da Lat Night Market open?

The night market starts bustling around 5pm and usually lasts until 4-5am the next morning. Evening is the most ideal time for you to immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere, watch the streets light up and enjoy delicious food.

![Da Lat night market]]

### 5.2 Where to park the car when going to Da Lat night market?

You can park your motorbike right at the main parking lot at the market gate for a fee of about 10,000 VND/trip. In addition, if this area is full, you can find a nearby parking lot such as the Da Lat market roundabout area, Tung Nghia parking lot (near Hôtel Colline hotel) or park your car at TASTY cafe and then walk to the market, which is also very convenient.

### 5.5 A few small notes to have a complete experience at Da Lat night market

- **Be careful with your belongings**: The night market is often crowded, especially on weekends or peak tourist seasons. You should wear a chest bag and avoid carrying a lot of cash or valuables.

- **Haggling skillfully**: Bargaining is very common at the market. Don't be afraid to negotiate to get the most reasonable price for the item you like.

- **Ask the price before eating**: The night market food area is quite vibrant, many restaurants do not clearly list prices. Therefore, you should carefully ask the price of the food before ordering to avoid being ripped off.

- **Check the quality of specialties**: If you plan to buy specialties such as jam, dried fruit or handicrafts as gifts, you should pay attention to the expiration date, packaging, color and flavor to choose a quality product.

- **Be careful when taking photos with mascots**: On weekends, many cartoon characters appear on the walking streets around the market. Although it looks very fun, you need to ask clearly about the cost of taking photos to avoid losing money unfairly.
