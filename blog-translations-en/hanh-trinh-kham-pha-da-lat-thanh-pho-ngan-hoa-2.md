# Journey to discover Da Lat - City of flowers




![Journey to discover Da Lat - City of flowers]]

Photo: Journey to discover Da Lat - City of thousands of flowers









Da Lat - a city of flowers, cool climate all year round - is a destination not to be missed for those who love traveling and want to enjoy a peaceful, romantic space. If you are planning a self-sufficient trip to Da Lat, please refer to the article below for the most useful experiences.

### 1. Choose the ideal time

Da Lat has a cool climate all year round, but each season has its own beauty:

🌸 **Spring (January - April):** The time when cherry blossoms bloom, the city is filled with pink.

🌞 **Summer (May - August):** Cool climate, suitable for trips to escape the sun.

🍂 **Autumn (September - November):** The season of bright yellow wild sunflowers and floating clouds.

❄️ **Winter (December):** Romantic with fog and cold air.

### 2. Transportation to Da Lat

✈️ **Airplane:** If you live far away, you can fly to Lien Khuong airport (about 30 km from Da Lat center). From the airport, you can take a taxi or bus to the center.

🚌 **Passenger cars:** Bus companies such as Phuong Trang and Thanh Buoi provide high quality sleeper bus services.

🛵 **Personal vehicle:** Riding a motorbike or personal car is a suitable choice for those who like to freely explore.

### 3. Choose accommodation

Da Lat has a variety of accommodation types, from homestays, hostels to luxury hotels. Some suggestions:

🏡 **Homestay:** Dalat 1900s, The Kupid – Dalat no Single.

🏨 **Hotel:** Terracotta Hotel & Resort, Dalat Palace Heritage Hotel.

🛏️ **Hostel:** Tigon Dalat Hostel, Yolo Hostel.

### 6. Some things to note when going to Da Lat on your own

👕 **Attire:** Dalat is quite cold in the early morning and evening, please bring a warm jacket. During the day you can wear it comfortably.

🛏️ **Book in advance:** Especially during peak season, you should book in advance to avoid being booked out.

🛵 **Plan your travel:** If you don't have a personal vehicle, rent a motorbike in Da Lat to conveniently explore.

🌿 **Environmental protection:** Please maintain hygiene at tourist destinations and do not pick flowers or break branches.
