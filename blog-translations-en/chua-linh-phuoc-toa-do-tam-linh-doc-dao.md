About 8km from Da Lat city center, Linh Phuoc Pagoda is the most famous spiritual destination thanks to its unique architecture inlaid with millions of pieces of porcelain and porcelain. Not only is it strangely beautiful, this place is also home to 11 national records, attracting thousands of tourists and Buddhists each year.

If you are planning to explore the mountain town, don't miss "Ve Chai Pagoda" - an artistic and spiritual symbol in the heart of Trai Mat.

## 2. History of formation and generations of Abbots

Construction of Linh Phuoc Pagoda began in 1949. Over many decades, especially under the reign of Venerable Thich Tam Vi (abbot from 1985 to present), the pagoda has been restored and expanded with its current unique appearance.

**List of typical abbots:**

-
Venerable Thich Minh The (1951 – 1954)

-
Venerable Thich An Hoa (1954 – 1956)

-
Venerable Thich Quang Phat (1956 – 1959)

-
Venerable Thich Minh Duc (1959 – 1985)

-
Venerable Thich Tam Vi (1985 – Present)

## 4. Explore unique architectural features

### 4.1. Hoa Long Vien yard and dragon encrusted with 12,000 bottles

As soon as entering the temple yard, visitors will be impressed with the 49m long dragon winding around the Maitreya Buddha statue. The entire dragon's body is crafted from **12,000 beer bottles**, creating an extremely eye-catching scene.

### 4.2. Linh Phap bell tower and Dai Hong Chung

The 7-storey bell tower (37m) is a prominent architectural highlight. Inside hangs the **Dai Hong Chung** weighing 8,500kg. Tourists often come here to write their wishes and paste them on the bell and invite the bell to pray for peace for their families.

### 4.3. Lady Buddha statue made of immortal flowers records record

Placed on the side of the main hall, the 18m high statue of Avalokiteśvara made from 650,000 immortal flowers is the pride of the pagoda. Every year the flowers are replaced to maintain their brilliance and solemnity.

### 4.4. Explore the 18 levels of hell at Ve Chai Pagoda

This is a project that attracts a lot of curiosity from tourists. With a length of more than 300m, **18 levels of hell** recreates the story of Muc Lien looking for his mother, while conveying profound lessons about cause and effect and filial piety.

## 5. Experience visiting Linh Phuoc Pagoda (Ve Chai Pagoda)

### 5.1. How to move

From Da Lat Market, you follow Tran Hung Dao - Hung Vuong street to Highway 20 towards Trai Mat. When you see the large Maitreya Buddha statue on your right, turn about 70 meters to the temple gate.

#### 5.2. Important notes

-
**Attire: **Because it is a sanctuary, you need to wear discreet, polite clothes (avoid short skirts and off-the-shoulder shirts).

-
**Time: **Should go in the morning (7am - 9am) to avoid crowds and get the best sunlight to take photos.

-
**Self-rotating table: **If you have time, visit the antiques display area to witness firsthand the mysterious and unsolved phenomenon of "self-rotating table".
