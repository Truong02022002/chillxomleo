### 1.3. Why is it called "Valley of Love"?

The Valley of Love is not only beautiful but also carries an interesting historical story. **During the French colonial period**, thanks to its mild climate and romantic terrain, this place was once a favorite dating spot for the French aristocracy.

During the reign of King Bao Dai, this place was renamed "Valley of Peace" to express the desire for harmony. However, in **1953**, the local government decided to rename it **“Valley of Love”**, to affirm the Vietnamese value and honor the inherent romantic beauty of this place.

Today, the name **“Valley of Love”** has become a prominent symbol of Da Lat. This place is also translated into English as **“The Valley of Love”**, is international in nature and attracts a large number of foreign tourists.

## 2. Map of Da Lat Valley of Love: Explore 3 outstanding areas

To make your tour at **Da Lat Valley of Love** more complete and convenient, understanding the subdivision map is essential. The tourist area is divided into **3 main areas**, each with its own unique features, making it easy for you to plan a reasonable exploration schedule.

### 2.1. Central area - The heart of the Valley of Love

This is the first area that visitors will set foot in when entering the tourist area gate. There are many delicately designed spaces for virtual check-ins, along with attractive art galleries.

- Coffee shops and beverage shops are scattered around so you can stop and relax in the middle of your journey.
- The area also has **light games**, suitable for both adults and children.
- Visitors can easily buy tickets, get directions maps, and receive advice right at the center.

### 2.2. Da Thien Lake area - Peaceful beauty in the heart of the valley

Continuing your journey from the center, you just need to go a little further and you will reach **Da Thien Lake** - a place considered as the green pearl of the Valley of Love. Here, you can participate in exciting activities such as:

- **Duck riding on the lake**– a romantic experience for couples.
- **Horse riding around the lake**– suitable for both adults and children.
- **Relaxing stroll** under the cool pine trees.

The space at Da Thien Lake is very suitable for enjoying peace and admiring the beautiful natural scenery.

### 2.3. Vong Canh Hill area - An ideal stop for fun and relaxation

Located in the highest area in the tourist area, **Vong Canh Hill** is a place loved by many tourists because of its spacious space and poetic scenery.

- This is the **ideal viewing spot**, giving you a panoramic view of the entire valley.
- Many **active and entertaining games** are arranged here, suitable for groups of friends or families who want to have fun moments.
- On beautiful days, you can catch golden rays of sunlight shining through the pine canopy, creating an extremely romantic scene.

## 3. What does Love Valley have? – 1001 interesting experiences not to be missed

Not only outstanding with its poetic natural scenery, Da Lat Valley of Love also offers a series of attractive activities for all ages. If you are wondering "What's there to do in Love Valley?**", then below are some unique experiences that you definitely should not miss.

### 3.1. Sightsee the valley by tram

One of the first activities when arriving at the tourist area is **traveling by electric vehicle**. The Valley of Love is like an outdoor film studio with countless **creative miniatures**, allowing you to freely pose and capture unique moments. Some outstanding landscapes include:

Da Thien Lake - a green gem in the middle of a pine forest - is the ideal place for you to relax, especially on a windy afternoon. While riding ducks, enjoying the fresh air and **romantic scenery**, this is an experience very popular with couples and families.

Right within the Valley of Love campus, you can visit **Dream Hill**– a place that stands out with flower beds blooming all year round. An exciting and meaningful experience for couples is **conquering the Love Maze**. Made up of thousands of meticulously trimmed green bushes, this maze is the most unique structure in Vietnam. ### 3.8. Enjoy the Central Highlands Gong culture

Not only does it have beautiful scenery, the Valley of Love is also a place that preserves unique cultural values. At the **Central Highlands Gongs stage** located in Mong Mo Hill area, you will enjoy traditional performances with **the sounds of gongs, stone instruments, flute sounds, dances**... in the mysterious, quiet space of the mountains and forests. The stage can accommodate up to 1,500 people, designed to simulate a unique fan architecture.
