# March 8 – Make a bet to Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop: Escape from the streets, touch peace




![March 8 – Make a bet to Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop: Escape from the streets, touch peace]]

Photo: March 8 – Make a bet to go to Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop: Escape the streets, touch peace







If you are looking for a place where you can temporarily leave the hustle and bustle of urban life to immerse yourself in nature, listen to the beat of the pine forest and enjoy the feeling of absolute relaxation, then **Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop** is a destination not to be missed. This is an ideal place to stop for those who love pristine Da Lat - rustic, peaceful but still attractive enough with very unique experiences.

## 1. Xom Leo Da Lat - A peaceful corner in the middle of a dreamy city

When mentioning Da Lat, many people immediately think of famous places such as Xuan Huong Lake, Valley of Love, or Lang Biang. But for those who love to explore and find new things, **Xom Leo Da Lat** is a special name. This is not only a place to "escape the city", but also an ideal chill spot to fully enjoy the pristine beauty of Da Lat.

![Leo Hamlet Da Lat]]

Photo: Xom Leo Da Lat

**Xom Leo** is a small residential area, isolated in the middle of a deep green pine forest, only about 15 minutes from the center of Da Lat. This place retains the wild character of old Da Lat, with stone roads, wooden houses with tiled roofs, bougainvillea trellises on the porch, and a strangely peaceful atmosphere.

If you are looking for a place to truly **chill in Da Lat**, then don't hesitate, **chill Xom Leo** is an experience you cannot miss.

### ⛺ Camping in the middle of the pine forest

![Da Lat camping]]

Photo: camping in Dalat

Coming to **Xom Leo Da Lat**, you cannot miss the experience of camping in the lush green pine forest. Bring a tent, set up camp and enjoy the chilly Da Lat night next to the open fire 🔥. Instead of a barbecue, try a more gentle chill style - sip a glass of **hot tea 🍵**, enjoy **hot milk 🥛**, relax in a peaceful space and immerse yourself in nature. Surely this will be a wonderful relaxing experience, helping you feel more deeply the peace of this land.

### ☁️ Hunt clouds early in the morning

![Cloud Hunting Da Lat]]

Photo: Cloud Hunting Da Lat

If you are a fan of cloud hunting, **Xom Leo** is also an ideal place. Early in the morning, you can climb to a high point to see the mist covering the entire landscape. This scene will definitely make you fascinated and want to save beautiful photos 📸.
