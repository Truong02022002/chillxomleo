Da Lat City of Thousands of Flowers is constantly renewing itself with many unique tourist destinations, attracting a large number of tourists from all over. One of the stops that has been "causing a fever" recently is Wonderland Da Lat - an entertainment area loved by many people thanks to its beautiful space and diverse activities suitable for all ages.

Here, you can experience countless exciting activities such as off-road driving, tubing, exploring the zoo and ostriches, and many other interesting games. Thanks to that, Wonderland becomes the ideal place for you to enjoy a full day of fun and relaxation amidst the fresh nature of Da Lat. If you are looking for a new destination for your journey to explore the mountain town, don't miss Wonderland!

1.****General information about Wonderland Da Lat

1.1. Location of Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Dalat Wonderland

Wonderland Da Lat is located on the campus of Da Lat Wonder Resort, with specific address at 19 Hoa Hong Street, Tuyen Lam Lake area, Ward 4, Da Lat city. This location is very convenient to combine relaxation and entertainment when coming to the foggy city.

1.2. Opening hours and ticket prices to visit Wonderland

Photo: Wonderland Da Lat

The amusement park Wonderland is open from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. every day. With many diverse activities, you can absolutely spend a whole day exploring and relaxing here.

Currently, Wonderland Da Lat applies two different ticket prices, depending on the type of tourists:

-

Guests staying at Da Lat Wonder Resort:

-

Adults:300,000 VND/ticket

-

Children (from 0.8 - 1.4m tall):150,000 VND/ticket

-

Non-stay guests (retail guests):

-

Adult:380,000 VND/ticket

-

Children (from 0.8 - 1.4m tall):190,000 VND/ticket

Children under 0.8m will receive free entrance tickets.

Note: This ticket price is full package, including all games in the amusement park. However, food and beverage services at the restaurant Salon De Thé will be charged separately, not included in the ticket price.

1.3. Map of Wonderland Da Lat amusement park

Photo: Da Lat wonderland map

Because Wonderland's area is quite large, visitors are encouraged to save the campus map to conveniently move around and not miss any exciting activities here. Thanks to the map, you can easily explore every area and experience a full day of fun at Wonderland.

2.****Instructions for traveling to Wonderland Da Lat

To get to Wonderland Da Lat, visitors can choose to travel by plane or bus, depending on the starting point and personal needs.

2.1. Travel by plane to ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Means of transportation to Wonderland Da Lat

Airplane is the fastest and most convenient means of transport if you depart from big cities such as Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Da Nang, etc. Flights will land at Lien Khuong airport, about 30km from Da Lat center. From the airport, you can take a taxi, service car, or public bus to get to the city center in about 30–40 minutes.

2.2. Travel by bus**** to ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Means of transportation to Wonderland Da Lat

In addition, if you want to save money or love long-distance trips, a bus is a reasonable choice. The route Saigon - Da Lat has many bus companies operating continuously throughout the day, with types such as single bed, double bed, or luxury limousine. Travel time ranges from 6 to 8 hours, suitable for both day and night trips.

2.3. From the center to Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Wonderland Zoo Dalat

Once you arrive in the center of Da Lat, you continue to move to No. 19 Hoa Hong Street, Tuyen Lam Lake, Ward 4 - where Wonderland Da Lat is located, only about 15 minutes by car from the center. Convenient road, can go by motorbike, car or taxi.

3. Wonderland Da Lat – What's so attractive about this "popular" amusement park?

Wonderland Da Lat is a diverse entertainment destination, suitable for families, groups of friends or couples looking for a day filled with experiences in the city of flowers. Let's explore what this amusement park has to offer!

3.1. The zoo****Wonderland Da Lat****close to nature

Photo: Excursing Tuyen Lam Lake by boat - Wonderland Da Lat

Here, you can meet many adorable animals such as sika deer, ostriches, puppies, suckling pigs, horses, and even peacocks, pheasants, canaries... The special thing is that you can also directly feed the animals, an activity that children will definitely enjoy and have the opportunity to learn more about the animal world.

If you're adventurous, try ostrich riding— a hair-raising but extremely memorable experience. If you like to relax, horseback riding around the campus is an equally gentle and romantic choice.

3.2. Cruise Tuyen Lam Lake by boat at ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Modern virtual reality game area Wonderland Da Lat

Enjoy the poetic scenery and fresh air by taking a boat ride around Tuyen Lam Lake. The quiet scenery, calm lake water and green nature will bring a valuable moment of silence amidst the busy pace of life.

3.3. Modern virtual reality game area at ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Dalat wonderland ice castle

The virtual reality game area with attractive games such as speed racing, watching 5D movies, action simulation... is where you can "live virtual technology" to the fullest. Modern space and diverse games make this place always attract many young people.

3.4. Ice castle****Wonderland Da Lat****only in Da Lat

Photo: Wonderland amusement park Dalat

A unique highlight at Wonderland is the ice castle cold to -4°C, with a 25m long ski slope. This is the ideal place for you to experience the feeling of "touching winter" right in the heart of Da Lat.

****

Photo: Dinosaur Park & Train 1979 Wonderland Da Lat

3.5. Fun paradise for children at ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Try your archery skills at Wonderland Da Lat

Wonderland owns both indoor and outdoor play areas specifically for children. Games such as slide, ball house, trampoline will keep children busy all day. A safe, colorful space helps children have fun and develop motor skills.

3.6. Dinosaur Park & Train 1979 at****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Wonderland Da Lat off-road vehicle

A journey on the 1979 vintage train will take you to dinosaur park, where there are lifelike models of prehistoric creatures. This is definitely an extremely hot and surprising "check-in" spot, especially for children or people who love fantasy movies.

3.7. Try your archery skills****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Ghost castle - a scary experience in Da Lat's wonderland

Do you want to try the feeling of being an archer? Wonderland offers exciting archery that will test your concentration and dexterity in a whole new setting.

3.8. Sleds & off-road vehicles – for speed enthusiasts at****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Dry float slide - refreshing and colorful wonderland Dalat

The two most "sought after" games at Wonderland are sleds and terrain vehicles. The feeling of gliding quickly through breathtaking turns in the cold weather will make your heart beat faster!

3.9. Ghost castle - a scary experience at ****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Promise Lake - a romantic corner for Dalat wonderland couples

Do you dare enter the spooky castle? The dark space, full of sounds and unexpected "scare" models will definitely make you scream more than once!

3.10. Dry tubing - refreshing and colorful at****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: 3D photography space & Wonderland virtual living miniatures

The slide has a slope of about 40 degrees and an eye-catching design that will give you the feeling of "flying". This is a game suitable for all ages and is very popular at Wonderland.

3.11. Wonderland Da Lat Promise Lake – a romantic corner for couples

Photo: Flower Garden & Avenue of love - European space in the heart of Da Lat

After having fun, stop at Lake of Promise. There is a statue of the God of Love here with a message of blessings for couples and hopes to bring good luck to single people.

3.12. 3D photography space & virtual living miniatures at****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Dining at wonderland

At Wonderland Da Lat, you can freely "relax" at the unique 3D photography area, along with hundreds of creative miniatures. From fairy tale castles to fantasy spaces, every corner is a place to create million-like photos.

3.13. Flower Garden & Avenue of Love****Wonderland Da Lat****– European space in the heart of Da Lat

Photo: Datanla Waterfall - Dalat wonderland

Wonderland also owns blooming flower gardens all year round, especially brilliant clusters of hydrangeas. Don't forget to visit Avenue of Love, where you can admire the poetic scenery of Da Lat Wonder Resort - considered a "miniature Europe" in the heart of the plateau.

4. Experience having fun at Wonderland Da Lat - Enjoy a full day without being boring

Are you about to have a trip to Wonderland Da Lat? Below are essential experiences to help you plan more fully and economically for your trip!

4.1. Buy tickets - Book in advance to save time when arriving****Wonderland Da Lat

Photo: Da Lat meatball sandwich - Dalat wonderland

You can buy tickets directly at the amusement park gate, however if you want to save waiting time, you should book tickets in advance through online ticketing platforms. In addition to avoiding queues, booking tickets early can also help you hunt for preferential prices, especially if combined with air tickets or hotel reservations.

4.2. What to eat at Wonderland Da Lat? – On-site restaurant

Photo: Terracotta Hotel & Dalat Resort - wonderland Dalat

Right in the Wonderland area is Salon De Thé restaurant, where you can have lunch after sightseeing and having fun. The menu here is diverse with prices ranging from 40,000 - 250,000 VND, suitable for many needs.

Not only is the food delicious, the restaurant space is beautifully designed, suitable for you to take advantage of "virtual living" with some real photos for your Da Lat travel photo collection.

5. Experience traveling to Da Lat at Wonderland Da Lat

5.1. Other destination suggestions****Wonderland Da Lat****when traveling to Da Lat

Photo: Grill & Chill Xom Leo - wonderland

In addition to Wonderland, Da Lat also possesses many other attractive destinations that you can combine in a long trip:

-

Datanla Waterfall– the ideal place to try slides or rope climbing adventures

-

Nobi Da Lat Town– a Japanese-style space in the heart of the plateau

-

Bao Dai Palace– ancient architecture marking the time of kings

-

Lake of Sighs– a poetic destination, associated with many love anecdotes

-

Da Lat ZooDoo– friendly zoo with semi-wild model

-

Puppy Farm– paradise for dog lovers and green farms

Small note: Many attractions in Da Lat are quite crowded during peak season, so you should book tickets online in advance to avoid wasting time queuing.

5.2. What's delicious to eat in Da Lat? – Don't miss the street food near****Wonderland Da Lat

Cuisine is an indispensable part of the journey to discover Da Lat. Below are typical dishes you should try at least once:

-

Da Lat grilled spring rolls– fragrant, fatty, served with extremely rolled raw vegetables

-

Banh Can– a crispy breakfast dish, often served with fish sauce

-

Shumai bread– hot, rich and easy to eat

-

Snails stuffed with meat– a typical favorite dish in cold weather

-

Xap Xap– Da Lat style beef jerky salad with a spicy flavor

-

Hot soy milk, cheese yogurt – specialties that are easy to find at night markets

Some places loved by tourists:

6. Suggested ideal restaurant after having fun at Wonderland Da Lat: Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop

After a day of exploring Wonderland Da Lat with all kinds of games from horseback riding, skiing to boating on Tuyen Lam Lake, you will surely want to find a chill place to "recharge your energy". And Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop is the perfect destination to end a perfect day.

Located on a small hill, the restaurant offers a very chill open space, where you can enjoy fragrant grilled dishes on a charcoal stove while watching the valley light up in the sunset. From the outdoor table area, you will see the looming glass roofs, sparkling light blending into the chilly fog - a very unique scene of Da Lat at night.

The restaurant has a diverse menu from grilled meat, grilled vegetables to hot hotpot, suitable for groups of friends, families or couples who want to find a romantic but still intimate space. The cozy, rustic yet stylish atmosphere is the reason why many tourists choose this place as a "stop" after a long journey.

If you are in need of an address that has delicious food, a beautiful view and a chill vibe after leaving Wonderland, don't miss Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop!
