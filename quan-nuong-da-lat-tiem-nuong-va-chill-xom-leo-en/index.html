Da Lat has long been a dream destination for not only young people but also those who love nature, explore and are passionate about food. The city on the plateau has a chilly atmosphere, green pine trees and a very unique romance that few places can match. But just having beautiful scenery is not enough.Da Latalso conquers tourists with its rich and unique cuisine, in which grill shopsDa Lat - especially places with a "chill" space - are always on the list of indispensable destinations.

And among hundreds of places, there is still one place that makes people remember forever from the first time they visit: *Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop*. This is not only a delicious barbecue restaurant, but also a space that meets all three factors: ideal location - cozy space - excellent food.

1. Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop - Da Lat grill restaurant with a unique combination of chill

Located at 113 Huynh Tan Phat, Ward 11, City. Da Lat, * Xom Leo Grill & Chill Shop * has gradually become a familiar destination for not only tourists but also local people when it comes to grilled restaurants in Da Lat. From the city center, it takes less than 15 minutes to get here. The location is easy to find, located near the main road, and has a view of the train passing by, creating an extremely poetic scene that not every restaurant has.

As soon as you set foot in the restaurant, you will feel a very different atmosphere: rustic, close and relaxing. The shop is designed in the "chill" style of Da Lat standards - open space, many trees, warm yellow light. Every small detail such as wooden tables and chairs, decorative lights, sparkling string lights or pretty little courtyard corners are all carefully cared for.

The restaurant is not simply a place to eat and drink, but also a place to enjoy the atmosphere, share moments, exchange emotionsbetween friends, relatives or even with yourself.
