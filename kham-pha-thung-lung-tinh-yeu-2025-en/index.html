Valley of Love is not simply a sightseeing destination but has become a characteristic symbol of Da Lat - the city of flowers. With poetic scenery and gentle air all year round, this place is always an ideal destination to relax, unwind and enjoy peaceful moments with nature.

Possessing the typical beauty of the Central Highlands mountains and forests, Valley of Lovemakes a strong impression on visitors with quiet lakes, stretching pine forests and colorful flower hills. Every corner here can become a beautiful background for million-heart check-in photos. That is also the reason why this place is always on the list of places not to be missed when traveling to Da Lat.

1.2. Road to the Valley of Love in Da Lat

Photo: Valley of Love

Moving to Valley of Love is quite simple thanks to the convenient road system and location near the center of Da Lat city. From Xuan Huong Lake, it only takes about 7km to get there, going north of the city.

- Moving instructions:

-

- Taxi/Grab: convenient, suitable for large groups

- Personal car: if you travel with your family

1.3. Why is it called "Valley of Love"?

Photo: Valley of Love

The Valley of Love is not only beautiful but also carries an interesting historical story. During the French colonial period, thanks to its mild climate and romantic terrain, this place was once a favorite dating spot for the French aristocracy.

During the reign of King Bao Dai, this place was renamed "Valley of Peace" to express the desire for harmony. However, in 1953, the local government decided to rename it "Valley of Love", to affirm the Vietnamese value and honor the inherent romantic beauty of this place.

Today, the name "Valley of Love" has become a prominent symbol of Da Lat. This place is also translated into English as "The Valley of Love", is international in nature and attracts a large number of foreign tourists.

2. Map of Da Lat Valley of Love: Explore 3 outstanding areas

To make your tour at Da Lat Valley of Love more complete and convenient, understanding the subdivision map is essential. The tourist area is divided into 3 main areas, each with its own unique features, making it easy for you to plan a reasonable exploration schedule.

2.1. Central area - The heart of the Valley of Love

Photo: Vong Canh Hill Da Lat - Valley of Love

This is the first area that visitors will set foot in when entering the tourist area gate. There are many delicately designed spaces for virtual check-ins, along with attractive art galleries.

- Coffee shops and beverage shops are scattered around so you can stop and relax in the middle of your journey.

- The area also has light games, suitable for both adults and children.

- Visitors can easily buy tickets, get directions maps, and receive advice right at the center.

2.2. Da Thien Lake area - Peaceful beauty in the heart of the valley

Photo: See the Valley of Love by electric car

Continuing your journey from the center, you just need to go a little further and you will reach Da Thien Lake - a place considered as the green pearl of the Valley of Love. Here, you can participate in exciting activities such as:

- Duck riding on the lake– a romantic experience for couples.

- Horse riding around the lake– suitable for both adults and children.

- Relaxing stroll under the cool pine trees.

The space at Da Thien Lake is very suitable for enjoying peace and admiring the beautiful natural scenery.

2.3. Vong Canh Hill area - An ideal stop for fun and relaxation

Photo: Valley of Love - 7D glass bridge

Located in the highest area in the tourist area, Vong Canh Hill is a place loved by many tourists because of its spacious space and poetic scenery.

- This is the ideal viewing spot, giving you a panoramic view of the entire valley.

- Many active and entertaining games are arranged here, suitable for groups of friends or families who want to have fun moments.

- On beautiful days, you can catch golden rays of sunlight shining through the pine canopy, creating an extremely romantic scene.

3. What does Love Valley have? – 1001 interesting experiences not to be missed

Not only outstanding with its poetic natural scenery, Da Lat Valley of Love also offers a series of attractive activities for all ages. If you are wondering "What's there to do in Love Valley?**", then below are some unique experiences that you definitely should not miss.

3.1. Sightsee the valley by tram

Photo: Miniature scene in the Valley of Love

One of the first activities when arriving at the tourist area is traveling by electric vehicle. With a large area, traveling by electric vehicle not only saves energy but is also an ideal way for you to admire the panoramic view of the valley in fresh, cool air. Tram tickets are included in your admission ticket, so you can enjoy it at your leisure.

3.2. Experience the 7D glass bridge

Photo: Miniature world in the Valley of Love

An expected highlight is the modern 7D glass bridge, connecting Dream Hill and Love Maze**. This will be the first glass-bottomed bridge in the Central - Central Highlands region, applying vivid image technology, promising to bring an extremely "heart-stopping" feeling and impressive check-in photos.

3.3. Live virtually freely with hundreds of miniature scenes

Photo: Duck Pedaling at Da Thien Lake, Valley of Love

The Valley of Love is like an outdoor film studio with countless creative miniatures, allowing you to freely pose and capture unique moments. Some outstanding landscapes include:

- Ancient European City

- Green tropical garden

- Giant chessboard miniature scene

- Gate of love, fairy tale castle, romantic flower lake

Whether you go with your loved one, your family or a group of friends, this place provides the ideal setting to "fire up".

3.4. Explore the model area of 30 wonders of the world

Photo: Dream Hill - Valley of Love

Have you ever dreamed of setting foot on famous structures such as The Great Wall, The Colosseumor The Leaning Tower of Pisa? Right in the Valley of Love, you can admire more than 30 famous wonders of the world through miniature versions that are exquisitely designed and vivid. The remote shooting angle also makes you feel like you are standing in the real world.

3.5. Relaxing duck ride on Da Thien lake

Photo: Dalat Valley of Love

Da Thien Lake - a green gem in the middle of a pine forest - is the ideal place for you to relax, especially on a windy afternoon. While riding ducks, enjoying the fresh air and romantic scenery, this is an experience very popular with couples and families.

3.6. Visit Mong Mo Hill filled with colorful flowers

Photo: Lamb Cuff Festival - Valley of Love

Right within the Valley of Love campus, you can visit Dream Hill– a place that stands out with flower beds blooming all year round. In particular, the blooming hydrangea season will make you unable to take your eyes off the dreamy green and purple fields. This is also the place most "hunted" by virtual living believers.

3.7. Test love at the largest tree maze in Vietnam

Photo: Da Lat

An exciting and meaningful experience for couples is conquering the Love Maze. Made up of thousands of meticulously trimmed green bushes, this maze is the most unique structure in Vietnam. Holding hands through the winding paths will definitely be memorable in the journey of you and your loved one.

3.8. Enjoy the Central Highlands Gong culture

Not only does it have beautiful scenery, the Valley of Love is also a place that preserves unique cultural values. At the Central Highlands Gongs stage located in Mong Mo Hill area, you will enjoy traditional performances with the sounds of gongs, stone instruments, flute sounds, dances... in the mysterious, quiet space of the mountains and forests. The stage can accommodate up to 1,500 people, designed to simulate a unique fan architecture.
